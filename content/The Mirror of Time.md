The Mirror of Time or sometime called the **[[The Mirror of Talia]]** is an unknown magical artifact created by the [[Talia the Weaver]] which is believe to be connected to the [[The Mirror of Time]], which allow anyone who wields it as is or within another object to be empowered beyond any magical items, as this mirror can access chronomancy and just pure [[Primordial Magical Energy]] which believe to be related to [[Memoria]], and [[Imaginatio]].

The Mirror of Time was split into nine pieces, according to unknown sources. Even so this fact is not very certain alongside this information is not known by most people, even those who are educated or [[Wayfarer]] of [[Talia the Weaver]]. As those who know this information believe that they should keep this information as hidden as possible for the security of this magical artifact.
