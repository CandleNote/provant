A **Dungeon Master (DM)** is the person who runs the Dungeons & Dragons game and makes the adventure happen.

They describe the world, tell players what they see, and play all the characters and creatures the players meet. The DM creates places to explore, quests to follow, and challenges to overcome. When players make choices, the DM changes the story to match what they do.

The DM also takes care of the rules. They decide when players roll dice, control the monsters in battles, and make sure the game stays fair and fun. Outside of fights, they help with talking to characters, solving puzzles, and exploring the world.

A Dungeon Master is part storyteller, part referee, and part guide — helping everyone have a fun time together.
