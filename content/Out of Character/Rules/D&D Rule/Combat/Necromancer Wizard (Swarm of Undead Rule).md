If a **Necromancer Wizard** controls **more than 3 Skeletons or Zombies** created by **[[Animate Dead (Spell)]]**, **2 undead fuse into 1 Swarm of Undead** (Skeleton or Zombie).

If **5 [[Animate Dead (Spell)]]** are active at once, they instead form **1 Swarm of Undead (Elite Skeleton or Elite Zombie)**.

---
#### Swarm of Undead (Skeleton)
_Medium Undead, Lawful Evil_
**AC** 14
**HP** 40
**Speed** 30 ft.
**Vulnerabilities** Bludgeoning
**Damage Immunities** Poison
**Condition Immunities** Exhaustion, Poisoned
**Cost :** 2 Skeleton from [[Animate Dead (Spell)]]

<u>Actions</u>
**Multiattack.** The skeleton makes two weapon attacks.
**Shortsword.** Melee Attack Roll: +5, reach 5 ft. Hit: 6 (1d6 + 3) Piercing damage.
**Shortbow.** Ranged Attack Roll: +5, range 80/320 ft. Hit: 6 (1d6 + 3) Piercing damage.

---
#### Swarm of Undead (Zombie)
_Medium Undead, Lawful Evil_
**AC** 8
**HP** 44
**Speed** 20 ft.
**Damage Immunities** Poison
**Condition Immunities** Exhaustion, Poisoned
**Cost :** 2 Zombie from [[Animate Dead (Spell)]]

**<u>Traits</u>**
**Undead Fortitude.** If damage reduces the zombie to 0 Hit Points, it makes a Constitution saving throw (DC 5 plus the damage taken) unless the damage is Radiant or from a Critical Hit. On a successful save, the zombie drops to 1 Hit Point instead.

<u>Actions</u>**
**Multiattack.** The zombie makes two weapon attacks.
**Slam.** _Melee Attack Roll:_ +3, reach 5 ft. _Hit:_ 5 (1d8 + 1) Bludgeoning damage.
