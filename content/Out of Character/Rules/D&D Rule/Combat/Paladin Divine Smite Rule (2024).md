The change to **[[Divine Smite]]** making it require a bonus action limits the Paladin’s smiting potential and reduces versatility, especially when combined with multi-attack.

If you are a full [[Paladin (Class)]] without any multiclassing, you can use [[Divine Smite]] without consuming a bonus action.

In addition, [[Divine Smite]] does not count toward the “one spell slot per action or bonus action” limitation. This applies to both pure [[Paladin (Class)]] characters and multiclass characters.
However, this does not apply to any version of [[Divine Smite]] that includes additional or special effects.
