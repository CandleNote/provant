Whenever you wish to jump, your maximum jump reach is equal to
- **Long Jump:** Move at least 10 feet to jump horizontally a distance equal to your **Strength score**. A standing long jump covers half that distance.
- **High Jump:** Move at least 10 feet to jump vertically up to (**3+Strength Modifier**) in feet. A standing high jump covers half that distance.

But if you're trying to **Long Jump** farther away than what you're able to do then you need roll athletic or acrobatic skill check equal to **8 + (Δ divide by 2)**
>    **For Example :** You are trying to long jump 20ft, your Strength score equal to 12, then your DC equal to 12 (8 + 8/2)

While for **High Jump**, you can make the same skill check equal to **10+Δ**
>    **For Example :** You are trying to high jump 10ft, your Strength score equal to 12 (+1), then your DC equal to 14 (10+4).
