Summons/Familiars hit point in Tales of Dasaron will have their maximum hit dice roll, so is easier for tracking sake, as every time a creature put down on the map. The HP of the creature get randomize.

**Example :**
**Owl HP** : 1d4-1 which mean the Owl have 4-1 = 3
**Riding Horse HP :** 2d10+2 which mean the Riding Horse Max HP have 20+2 = 22
