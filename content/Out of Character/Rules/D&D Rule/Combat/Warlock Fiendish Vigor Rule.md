In _Tales of Dasaron_, **Fiendish Vigor** gains the following special rule:

If you are a **full Warlock** (meaning you have not multiclassed into any other class), the spell granted by this invocation is cast at a level equal to your Pact Magic slot level.

This rule exists to improve scaling and maintain the invocation’s usefulness at higher levels.

Casting this spell still follows all normal rules:
- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Components:** Verbal, Somatic, and Material (a drop of alcohol or an arcane focus)
Eldritch Invocations do not bypass component requirements unless explicitly stated.
