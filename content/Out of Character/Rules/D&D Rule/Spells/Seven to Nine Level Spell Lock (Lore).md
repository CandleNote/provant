Spells of Seventh (7th) to Ninth (9th) Level cannot be learned directly through leveling up or by any normal means. Instead, they must be obtained during the campaign through story progression, discoveries, rewards, training, or other events within the session.

This rule is designed to help balance the game by limiting access to the exceptionally powerful spells that exist at these levels.

Characters still gain **access to their 7th-, 8th-, and 9th-level spell slots** as normal. However, possessing these spell slots does not automatically grant knowledge of spells of those levels. A character must first acquire the spell in session/game before they can prepare, learn, or cast it.
