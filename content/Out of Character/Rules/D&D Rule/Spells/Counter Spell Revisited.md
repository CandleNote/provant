[[Counter Spell]] (3rd-level spell) uses the 2014 version of [[Counter Spell]] for all player characters, allowing them to make an Ability Check when attempting to counter a spell cast at a higher level than their own [[Counter Spell]].

Additionally, the DC for this Ability Check is calculated using the following formula:

> **DC = 13 + Target Spell Level - Your Counter Spell Level**
#### Example
An enemy casts [[Cone of Cold]] (5th-level spell), and you respond with a 4th-level [[Counter Spell]].
> 13 + 5 - 4  
> = 18 - 4  
> = 14

The Ability Check DC would therefore be **14**.

This change is intended to make higher-level castings of [[Counter Spell]] more valuable, even when they are not cast at a high enough level to automatically negate the target spell.

### For Enemies :
[[Counter Spell]] for enemies follows the 2014 version, so you need to roll a Constitution Saving Throw. As with the previous ruling, I want both the player and the enemy to roll and compare their results to determine who succeeds and who fails.
