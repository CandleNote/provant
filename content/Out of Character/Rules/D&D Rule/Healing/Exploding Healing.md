**Exploding Healing** works the same way as exploding dice, but for healing.
When you roll healing dice (such as from a spell, potion, or ability), **if a die rolls its maximum value**, you roll that die again and **add the new result** to the healing total.  
This can repeat as long as you keep rolling the maximum number.
### Example
- You heal **1d8**
- You roll an **8** → roll another **1d8**
- You roll a **5**
- Total healing = **13**
### Why We Use This Rule
- Makes healing feel more impactful and exciting
- Gives clutch moments when luck matters
- Helps reduce bad-feeling low healing rolls

This rule only applies to **healing dice**, not damage, unless stated otherwise by the [[Dungeon Master]].
