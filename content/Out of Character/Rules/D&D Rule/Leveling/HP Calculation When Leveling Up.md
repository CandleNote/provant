Whenever your level up, you gain hit point equal to a highest roll between two of your hit dice, although if your highest roll is lower than average then use the average.
We still make you keep the maximum for your first level. This system is so when we calculate for hit point is a lot easier.

**Example 1 :**
Fighter 4 -> 5
Dice roll (1d10) : 9, and 3
Average of d10 : 6
You keep = 9

**Example 2 :**
Fighter 4 -> 5
Dice roll (1d10) : 4, and 3
Average of d10 : 6
You keep = 6
