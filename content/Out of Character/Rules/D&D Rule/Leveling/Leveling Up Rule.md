_Tales of Dasaron_ uses a **custom leveling system** inspired by **D&D’s Milestone Level Up system**. The primary method of advancement is **Milestone Points**, though **standard milestone leveling** may be used in some campaigns.

**Milestone Points** track a character’s **progress toward leveling up**. When a character gains **enough Milestone Points**, they **advance one level**.

**Milestone Points** are mainly earned by completing **[[Mission & Quest (OoC)|Missions/Quests]]**. The Dungeon Master may also award points for **major story arcs**, **significant narrative achievements**, or **world-impacting actions**.
