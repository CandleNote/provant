Major Arcana are Arcana that is use by [[Player Character]] and some very important NPCs like Main Villain or ally which help shape the story of the campaign.
### 0. **[[The Fool (0)]]** 🤍
**Upright:** innocence, new beginnings, free spirit  
**Reversed:** recklessness, carelessness, being misled
### I. **[[The Magician (I)]]** ✨
**Upright:** willpower, creation, manifestation  
**Reversed:** trickery, illusions, lost potential
### II. **[[The High Priestess (II)]]** 🌙
**Upright:** intuition, inner voice, hidden truth  
**Reversed:** confusion, emotional suppression, lost guidance
### III. **[[The Empress (III)]]** 🌿
**Upright:** creation, nurture, growth, life  
**Reversed:** dependence, neglect, emotional emptiness
### IV. **[[The Emperor (IV)]]** 👑
**Upright:** authority, order, leadership  
**Reversed:** tyranny, control, emotional coldness
### V. **[[The Hierophant (V)]]** 📜
**Upright:** tradition, belief, guidance  
**Reversed:** rebellion, broken traditions, new paths
### VI. **[[The Lovers (VI)]]** ❤️
**Upright:** unity, bonds, meaningful choices  
**Reversed:** imbalance, separation, broken trust
### VII. **[[The Chariot (VII)]]** 🛡️
**Upright:** determination, victory, control  
**Reversed:** aggression, lack of direction, defeat
### VIII. **[[Strength (VIII)]]** 🦁
**Upright:** courage, patience, inner power  
**Reversed:** doubt, fear, weakness
### IX. **[[The Hermit (IX)]]** 🕯️
**Upright:** reflection, wisdom, solitude  
**Reversed:** isolation, confusion, lost purpose
### X. **[[Wheel of Fortune (X)]]** 🔄
**Upright:** fate, change, destiny  
**Reversed:** bad luck, resistance to change
### XI. **[[Justice (XI)]]** ⚖️
**Upright:** truth, balance, fairness  
**Reversed:** lies, injustice, denial
### XII. **[[The Hanged Man (XII)]]** ⛓️
**Upright:** sacrifice, new perspective, acceptance  
**Reversed:** stagnation, fear of change
### XIII. **[[Death (XIII)]]** ☠️
**Upright:** endings, transformation, rebirth  
**Reversed:** resistance, stagnation, fear of loss
### XIV. **[[Temperance (XIV)]]** ⚗️
**Upright:** balance, harmony, patience  
**Reversed:** excess, imbalance, recklessness
### XV. **[[The Devil (XV)]]** 🔥
**Upright:** desire, temptation, bondage  
**Reversed:** freedom, release, breaking chains
### XVI. **[[The Tower (XVI)]]** 🌩️
**Upright:** destruction, sudden change, revelation  
**Reversed:** disaster avoided, fear of change
### XVII. **[[The Star (XVII)]]** ⭐
**Upright:** hope, healing, faith  
**Reversed:** despair, loss of faith
### XVIII. **[[The Moon (XVIII)]]** 🌕
**Upright:** illusion, fear, hidden truths  
**Reversed:** clarity, truth revealed
### XIX. **[[The Sun (XIX)]]** ☀️
**Upright:** joy, success, warmth  
**Reversed:** sadness, delay, false happiness
### XX. **[[Judgement (XX)]]** 🔔
**Upright:** awakening, reckoning, renewal  
**Reversed:** denial, refusal to change
### XXI. **[[The World (XXI)]]** 🌍
**Upright:** completion, unity, fulfillment  
**Reversed:** incompletion, lack of closure
