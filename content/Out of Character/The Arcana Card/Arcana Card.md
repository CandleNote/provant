The **Arcana(s)** are powerful buffs gained when a deep connection forms between two or more individuals. They awaken when people grow closer through :
- Reaching a true goal that one desire
- Reaching a major understanding with another person
- Facing a turning point in a life
- Experiencing loss, success, or failure
- Face destiny together

This phenomenon is explained by **[[The Ambition Theory]]**, where strong ambition and mutual desire cause one’s will to manifest as power. When this bond reaches a turning point, both individuals involved become empowered.

This turning of an Arcana does not always mean that the connection between two or more people grows in a good way. It simply means that they understand more about one another, and about themselves, which creates a strong ambition.

This ambition is what causes the Arcana to awaken. It may come from trust, conflict, realization, or change. Whether the result is good or bad depends on the people involved, not the Arcana itself.

---
There are **22 [[Major Arcana]]** and **56 Minor Arcana**, each granting a different effect based on the nature of the bond that created it.
