The [[Yunaka Foundation]], ruled over by the [[Yunaka Family]], is a powerful corporation that holds influence across the [[Continent of Vallarian]]. Once renowned magical smiths from [[Mondral]], they transformed their craft into mass production, building an empire that now dominates countless industries. This domination has caused many to believe that the [[Yunaka Foundation]] is involved in criminal activities, but they have become far too large to fail, as countless livelihoods depend upon them and many politicians are connected to their influence. Yet it is not only politicians who are rumored to stand beside them. It is believed that criminals, too, are tied to this vast web.

A task has been offered to any who are willing by an unknown group that refuses to reveal its name, promising an immense payment in exchange for investigating several mountain villages that mysteriously vanished after a meteor shower passed above them nearly 120 years ago during [[The Great Vallarian War]]. At the time, the war itself made such investigations meaningless, and no one seemed interested in uncovering the truth. Until now. For it seems the [[Yunaka Foundation]], the [[Ouroboros Organization]], and the unknown group that hired you have all suddenly turned their gaze toward these forgotten villages.

Some believe these villages are now nothing more than ruins, abandoned through depopulation and war. Others whisper that aberrations from beyond the skies took them, for not a single villager was ever seen leaving, and due to their isolated locations, the villages were simply cast aside and forgotten by the rest of the world.

There must be something more that remains within these so-called missing villages. The attention of two massive organizations has fallen upon the mountainside without warning. There must be a reason the pieces upon this chessboard have begun to move once more. Yet only action can turn the page of this tale, for it remains shrouded in darkness.
