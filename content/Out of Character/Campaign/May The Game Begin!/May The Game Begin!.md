You and several others have been hired by the military of Alvavia, along with the Peace Keepers from the [[Union of Vallarian Nation]], to investigate the [[City of Lightfield]] within Alvavia. For reasons unknown, the once bustling city has gone completely silent. For the past month, no one has been seen leaving, and anyone who enters the city’s territory is never seen again. This phenomenon began at the same time a massive golden tree suddenly appeared in the heart of the city, observed only from a distance. Many believe it to be the cause.

In response, the Alvavian military dispatched General Friedrich Karl along with 500 men to investigate. None of them returned. After four weeks without any signal or contact from the expedition, the Peace Keepers deemed the situation too dangerous to ignore. The disappearance of an entire city, alongside a military force, posed a threat too great to remain contained. They assembled a group of adventurers and mercenaries to uncover the truth behind what is happening in the [[City of Lightfield]]. Recently, a new clue emerged: a strange, flat metal device with a glass front was recovered from a corpse found in the forest near the city. It appears to have unusual buttons along its side.

This is where you come in. 
You have been recruited for a covert investigation into the [[City of Lightfield]], tasked with uncovering the fate of General Friedrich Karl and his men. The mission is classified, as the Peace Keepers wish to avoid public panic. For now, you and the others have been instructed to meet in a secluded tavern deep within the forest.
What awaits you remains uncertain. Many questions are still unanswered, but it is only a matter of time before the truth is revealed.
