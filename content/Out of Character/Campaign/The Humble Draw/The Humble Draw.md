The campaign is set in the humble mountain [[City of Goldencaster]], a place known for its magical knowledge, arts, and wealth. It stands on the mountainside of The Golden Mountains, where much of the gold in the Kingdom of Kastugar, and even the [[Continent of Vallarian]], is found. Yet you are not here to simply wander. You are here for what was meant to be a simple mission: guarding a nobleman who is also a famous artist.

[[Lysander Vérité]], known as the man who can draw things that feel real, has been receiving death threats from an unknown source. You, as adventurers or mercenaries, have agreed to this quest for magical knowledge, goods, or simply gold. The mission came to you as you passed through town, perhaps at a tavern or through a friend. The payment is large, for Lysander promises two tasks: first, to protect him at an art showcase, and afterward, to uncover who threatens his life. He will pay well, even if he is a pariah among nobles and in his own family.

So, adventurers, you have taken this chance for knowledge, glory, or wealth. What will happen on your journey? What will occur along the way, as darker matters stir than you expect? Only the turn of a page, where a bird may yet feel the breeze of free air... could reveal such things, friend.
## **Tone :** 
> Y'all will be playing the heroic side, so please don't make your character as someone who will kill everyone for petty reason. You can make a neutral character or doing it for selfish reason. But no for no reason, like being the super villain.
