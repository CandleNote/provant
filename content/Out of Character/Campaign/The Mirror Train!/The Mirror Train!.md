In the **[[City of Yoshino]]**, [[Mondral]], the **[[Church of Talia]]** has invited all of you on a simple quest to explore the nearby **[[Sorcerer Forest]]**, located just outside the city. However, the reason for the investigation is quite strange. It seems that a piece of the **[[Talia the Weaver|Silent Goddess]]’s** collection of magical goods, specifically **[[The Mirror of Talia]]**, has been shattered, and it is now the duty of the **[[Church of Talia]]** to search for the fragments.

Strangely, this knowledge did not come from any **[[Celestial]]** being, but instead from an unknown source that can only be described as a **[[Construct (Type)|Construct]]**. This Construct appears to possess a train, an expensive and unusual asset for a being with no known background. Yet there is no time to question these mysteries. The Construct has willingly offered its help, and all agree that if **[[The Mirror of Time]]** were to fall into the wrong hands, the consequences would be disastrous.

The **[[Church of Talia]]** has called a meeting at the church of **[[Kagri, the God of Knowledge, Sailing, and Undead]]**, near **[[City of Yoshino]] Station**. What awaits within this tale remains uncertain. Many questions are still unanswered, but it is only a matter of time before the truth is revealed.
