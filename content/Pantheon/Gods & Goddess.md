Gods and Goddesses are terms mortals use to refer to immortal beings who possess control over some form of [[Divine Magical Energy]]. Their power exists because people believe in them, or believe in the [[Divine Epithet]] that the God or Goddess embodies. Some may follow these beings only partially and not devote themselves fully to that path, while others fully embrace it. Through faith and belief, mortals naturally grant [[Divine Magical Energy]] toward the Gods and Goddesses, as belief itself creates and channels magical energy through devotion.
### Types of Gods and Goddess
There are three types of Gods and Goddess that exist in the [[Multiverse]], which are [[Primordial Gods|Primordial Pantheon]], [[Planar Gods|Planar Pantheon]], [[Vallarian Gods and Goddess|Vallarian Pantheon]], and [[Druidic Pantheon]]. Each different types describe where their power come from, and what [[Divine Epithet]] and [[Divine Oath]] that their [[Wayfarer]] would follow.

### Mortality of The Gods
Gods and Goddesses possess [[Immortality]], but they are not truly unkillable. 

One way to destroy a god is by eroding the faith of their followers, weakening them until they can finally die. However, this is extremely difficult, as most gods possess countless believers. Unlike true gods, [[Demi-Gods]] possess only limited amounts of [[Divine Magical Energy]], making them far easier to eliminate.

The second method is through direct conflict with another god-level being. Such battles are rare, as [[Lord Death]] ensures order is maintained and prevents open divine warfare with the [[Pact of Lord Death]].
