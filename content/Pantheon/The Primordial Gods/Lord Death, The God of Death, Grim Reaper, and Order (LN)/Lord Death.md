**Lord Death, the God of Death, Grim Reaper, and Order,** is one of the strangest beings to exist in the multiverse. He serves as the arbiter of those known as [[Gods & Goddess]], and, like the other [[Primordial Gods]] such as [[Talia the Weaver]], he has existed since the very beginning of the multiverse itself. However, unlike [[Talia the Weaver]], he does not possess a large following. Those who do follow him are typically devoted to his [[Divine Epithet]] associated with order.

His [[Wayfarer]] believe that order must be upheld to push back against chaos, which would otherwise bring the multiverse closer to destruction. This idea aligns with what is known as the natural order of existence, often referred to as [[Entropy Theory]]. Because of this, balance and order are considered essential.

This belief is most commonly expressed through the destruction of undead beings. Their existence is seen as a disruption to the balance of the planes, according to [[Entropy Theory II]]. The greater the number or power of undead in a single location, the higher the risk that the structure of that plane may collapse.

Followers of Lord Death also take pride in the belief that the desire for freedom is universal among all living beings. As such, they strive to grant freedom wherever possible. For those who oppose this ideal or seek to create a world without such freedom, it becomes the duty of the Wayfarers of Lord Death to ensure that such a path never comes to pass.
#### Symbols :
The symbols of Lord Death are twofold. One is the **Lycoris (Red Spider Lily)**, which, in [[Titanfall Isle]] and the eastern side of the [[Continent of Vallarian|Land of Valla]], has become strongly associated with death and with Lord Death himself. Many Wayfarers craft this symbol into amulets, while some attempt to use the real flower, though this is rare due to maintenance and practical limitations.

The other common symbol is the **skull**, sometimes depicted with bones and sometimes alone. These are typically crafted into amulets or banners, though real bones are not used.
#### Origin :
The origin of Lord Death is largely unknown. However, it is believed that he is a primordial being formed from [[Primordial Magical Energy]], born directly as a god. This nature makes his decisions and actions appear highly amoral, though they are ultimately intended to preserve the longevity of existence itself.

Some documents discovered during the [[Calamity Era]] suggest that Lord Death was not alone in origin. There may have been two other beings born of [[Primordial Magical Energy]] like him. One remains unknown, while the other is known as the [[Enemy of Creation]], who was fought during the [[Calamity Event]] by many forces, including Lord Death himself.

#### Tenents of Death :
Followers of Lord Death are expected to uphold these principles:
- **Life & Death must flow** — it may be observed, but never controlled by anyone.
- **One to rule them all is a lie** — the future beholden by everyone, not by one person.
- **Action must be look upon before** — to act without understanding consequence is to invite fracture.
#### Power of Lord Death :
The full extent of Lord Death’s power is not well understood. However, it is widely believed that no being mortal or divine being can escape death, reinforcing his status as an immensely powerful deity. Despite this, he tends to remain distant from most events across different eras, leaving matters largely to his Wayfarers.

One of his greatest displays of power was the creation of the [[Pact of Lord Death]] following the [[Calamity Era]]. Through this pact, he and the other gods across the multiverse agreed to refrain from directly interfering in mortal affairs, offering only indirect aid such as blessings. Curiously, this restriction does not seem to apply to [[Talia the Weaver]].
#### Nickname :
**The Order Keeper** : a rarely used title, as most simply refer to him as Lord Death. This name originates from earlier eras, reflecting his divine authority in maintaining order and balance throughout the multiverse.

#### Holy Laws & Taboo
**Holy Laws**
- **Life and Death must flow** — neither should be halted nor denied.
- **Order preserves existence** — maintain balance so chaos does not consume the world.
- **Death is not an enemy** — it is a necessary part of existence and must be accepted.
- **Act with understanding** — every action has consequences upon the greater order.
- **Protect the natural cycle** — all things must have a beginning and an end.

**Taboos**
- **Creating or sustaining unnatural undeath** is forbidden.
- **Seeking [[Immortality]] at the expense of the natural order** is condemned.
- **Placing oneself above the cycle of life and death** is an act of arrogance.
- **Spreading chaos for its own sake** threatens the balance of existence.
- **Claiming dominion over life and death** is forbidden, for no mortal or god has such authority.
