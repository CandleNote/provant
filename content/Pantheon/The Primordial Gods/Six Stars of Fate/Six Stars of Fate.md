The Six Stars of Fate are one of the strangest [[Gods & Goddess]] to exist within the Multiverse. Unlike other [[Gods & Goddess]], they are believed to have originated from six different groups of heroes who fought against the [[Enemy of Creation]] during the [[Calamity Era]] and vanished during the [[Calamity Event]]. Although the heroes themselves are gone, their message of fighting against one's fate and forging a brighter future, even against those far more powerful than oneself, remains strong among their [[Wayfarer]].

Even if the heroes behind the Six Stars of Fate are gone and believed to have perished during the [[Calamity Event]], the [[Six Colour Stars]] within the [[Astral Sea]] are believed to be more closely connected to the Six Stars of Fate than anything else. Furthermore, most [[Wayfarer]] of the Six do not believe in the need for a pantheon to place their faith in, as the Six Stars instead emphasize belief in oneself and one's companions when facing a predetermined fate. This philosophy is often associated with [[Ambition Theory]], which teaches that those with the ambition to fight against their fate can reach the highest limits of power.
#### Symbols :
The symbols of the [[Six Stars of Fate]] are the **Six Stars Symbol** and the **[[Flower of Fate]]**. The Six Stars Symbol is a six-pointed star formed from six different colors, representing the six heroes and the ideals they left behind. It is commonly worn as a pendant, engraved onto equipment, or displayed upon shrines and banners dedicated to the Six Stars.

The **[[Flower of Fate]]** is a rare six-petaled lily whose petals bear the same six colors as the [[Six Colour Stars]]. Among [[Wayfarer]], it symbolizes hope, ambition, and the belief that fate can be challenged. Artistic depictions of the flower are far more common than the real flower itself due to its extreme rarity.
#### Origin
The Six Stars were originally six groups of heroes. Within each group was an individual who possessed one of the special Eyes of the Six, more commonly known as [[The Eye of Galaxy]]. Together, they fought against the [[Enemy of Creation]], aided by countless other heroes and even former villains whom they encountered throughout their journey.

Although they suddenly vanished and are widely believed to have perished during the [[Calamity Event]], their legacy endured. Those who heard their wishes and teachings of fighting against fate itself became known as their [[Wayfarer]]. Over time, their words spread across the Multiverse, inspiring many who had fallen into despair or were on the verge of giving up to change their path and strive to become something greater.

As generations passed, the stories of these heroes gradually evolved into a faith centered around hope, ambition, and the belief that one's destiny is never truly fixed. Eventually, the six heroes came to be revered as the Six Stars of Fate.
#### Tenets of the Six Stars of Fate :
Followers of the Six Stars of Fate are expected to uphold these principles:
- **Fate is not absolute** — no path is predetermined, and every future can be changed through one's actions.
- **No one walks alone** — greatness is not achieved by oneself alone, but through the bonds forged with companions.
- **Leave the world brighter than before** — every action should bring hope to others and create a better future for those who follow.
#### Power
The Six Stars of Fate possess little to no direct divine power, as the heroes behind the Six have never appeared again following the [[Calamity Event]]. Despite this, the [[Wayfarer]] of the Six do not rely on power granted by their patrons. Instead, their strength comes from their own ambition, determination, and unwavering desire to achieve their goals.

The teachings of the Six Stars hold that those who possess a dream worth pursuing and the resolve to see it through can draw upon a power far greater than themselves. As a result, many Wayfarers experience sudden surges of ambition, hope, and strength during their darkest moments, allowing them to push beyond what they once believed were their limits.

For this reason, many scholars debate whether the power of the Six Stars truly comes from the Six themselves, or from the collective will of those who continue to follow their ideals.
#### Nickname
Like many gods, Six Stars of Fate is known by several names given by mortals and their [[Wayfarer]]
- **The Six** : This nickname is just a simple shorter version of the Six Star of Fate.

#### Holy Laws & Taboo
**Holy Laws**
- **Fate is meant to be challenged** — no destiny is absolute, and every path can be changed.
- **Ambition must be pursued** — a dream without action is merely a wish.
- **Stand against the impossible** — even overwhelming odds are not a reason to surrender.
- **Walk your own path** — your future belongs to you, not to prophecy or expectation.
- **Strength is found together** — companions and bonds are as important as personal ambition.
- **Leave hope behind** — your actions should inspire others to strive for a better future.

**Taboos**
- **Forcing destiny upon others** is forbidden.
- **Stealing another's ambition** through coercion, manipulation, or oppression is condemned.
- **Surrendering to despair** when a path still exists is seen as a rejection of the Six's teachings.
- **Claiming fate is absolute** denies the core belief of the Six Stars.
- **Abandoning companions for personal glory** betrays the bonds that allowed the Six to succeed.
- **Using power to crush dreams and aspirations** is considered a grave sin.
