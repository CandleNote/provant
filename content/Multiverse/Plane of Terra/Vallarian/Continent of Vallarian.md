Vallarian or sometimes simply called as Valla is the continent that exist in the [[Plane of Terra]] or some might call the [[Plane of Terra|Material Plane]]. The Vallarian continent exists due to the Guardians (commonly known as the [[Guardians of Titanfall]]), which are [[Demi-Gods]] from the [[Continent of Titanfall]], who wished not to join the [[Titanfall Trial]] as they did not wish to kill other Guardians, or due to self-interest, or simply curiosity about the plans that some of the other [[Guardians of Titanfall]] were preparing. So they combined their [[Primordial Magical Energy]] together to melt the earth and create a new continent that was named Vallarian after the Guardians who created it, who were also named the Vallarian.
## Geography
Valla itself has many different and strange geographical features compared to Titanfall, as the [[Geography of Vallarian]] can be calming and peaceful, or what people would call normal. Although sometimes this normality changes due to strange occurrences that have happened throughout the long history of Vallarian, which altered the environment around them.

In addition, the geography of the land of Valla may be the result of the unnatural creation of Valla by the [[Progenitor of Valla]] and the [[Initiator of Valla]], who created the continent rather than it forming naturally. One of the most famous geographical creations by these Guardians are the [[Northern Peaks]], [[Midland Peaks]], and [[Southern Peaks]].

Although the modern-day [[Sea of Calamity]], which splits the [[Northern-land of Vallarian]] and the [[Mid-land of Vallarian]], used to be a simple giant sea, after the [[Calamity Event]] which occurred during the [[Calamity Era]], the [[White Night Sea]] later became the [[Sea of Calamity]]. The [[Calamity Event]] also caused a magical shift within the sky of Valla, as it seems the continent was hit with the [[Shattered Sky]], which changed the perspective of most people, allowing them to see the [[Astral Sea]] or what some might call Outer Space.

Around the continent, just like most other continents that exist in the [[Plane of Terra|Material Plane]], it is surrounded by the [[Sea of Leviathan]], which makes multi-continental travel almost impossible, as the [[Leviathan]] within the deep sea would destroy any ships that travel too far from shore. This makes the knowledge, communication, and expedition between continents almost nonexistent, other than in rare occurrences.

## History of Vallarian
The continent of Vallarian has a long and complicated history, which is usually split into several Eras by most [[Historian]] within the normal academic field or the [[Intelligentsia Guild]]. Many [[Nations of Valla]] have fought against one another for power or wealth, or due to simple petty arguments—as many mortals tend to do.

These events shaped the entire continent’s course of history. Of course, some people who follow the [[Second Herrschers]] theory of [[The Ambition Theory]] believe that these events occurred due to chains of actions caused by ambitious individuals clashing with one another, empowered, Whether knowingly or not, by the [[Six Stars of Fate]].

### Vallarian Creation
The creation of the Continent of Vallarian occurred only because of the [[Progenitor of Valla]] and the [[Initiator of Valla]], who were [[Guardians of Titanfall]] seeking to escape the [[Third Titanfall Trials]] for reasons that varied from one to another. The most powerful among them were called the [[Progenitor of Valla]], comprising sixteen notably powerful [[Guardians of Titanfall]]. Those who were not counted among the sixteen were simply called the [[Initiator of Valla]], whose true number remains unknown to the public.

But one thing is certain: they combined their powers through the use of the immense [[Primordial Magical Energy]], a force possessed only by some [[Demi-Gods]] and believed to reside within the [[Core of Terra]], from which the [[Guardians of Titanfall]] draw their strength. Through this combined might, they were able to part the surrounding sea and shape new land. Thus rose the Continent of Vallarian—now simply called Valla—an event recorded to have taken place around 9256 [[After Lament of Fallen (AF)|After Lament of Fallen (AF)]], according to most surviving chronologies.

### The Guardians Squabble
After 3 years of the creation of the Vallarian Continent. Two of [[Guardians of Titanfall]] decided to travel towards Valla after hearing what occurred and beating the Guardians that threated their territories, which [[Dewi Danu]], and [[Ongon-Ongon]] heard from the other [[Guardians of Titanfall]] who were in the fence about killing the [[Progenitor of Valla]] and [[Initiator of Valla]], but these two were impatient and decided to travel towards Valla.

Although not as expected for [[Dewi Danu]], and [[Ongon-Ongon]]. The united front from the [[Progenitor of Valla]], and [[Initiator of Valla]] make them have to work for their right to claim over Vallarian. Which end with their death. 

## Etymology
The etymology of the continent is quite simple, as the continent of Valla is named after the 16 major [[Guardians of Titanfall]], who are called the [[Progenitor of Valla]]. These [[Demi-Gods]] became a group called the Vallarian, a name given by the followers/[[Wayfarer]] of these Guardians who believed in their Guardians enough to leave their homeland of Titanfall to seek a new life in Vallarian. This was not simply due to blind faith, as many scholars believe that these people left their home not only because of belief, but also because of how horrible the [[Third Titanfall Trials]] were toward the mortal population.
