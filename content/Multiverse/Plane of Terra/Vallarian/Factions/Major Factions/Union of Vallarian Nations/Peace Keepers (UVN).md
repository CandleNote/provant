Peace Keepers are a military group created by the [[Union of Vallarian Nation]] with the goal of protecting civilians and keeping the peace during wartime. These units differ from groups like the [[Holy Order]] military, as they do not have any goal of victory or active combat other than defensive actions.

To add, the Peace Keepers have a unique style of recruiting their soldiers and generals. Their soldiers are usually individuals who are already in the military, which the [[Nations of Valla]] decide to send as a goodwill gesture. However, the generals are usually [[Adventurers]] or military personnel who have chosen to leave their previous positions to join the Peace Keeper Group, as the [[Union of Vallarian Nation]] has decided that the [[General of the Peace Keeper]] should not be tied to any single nation. [[Adventurers]] who join are typically not [[Patriotic]] toward any nation, and military personnel who leave are often similarly neutral.

However, this does not always hold true throughout history, due to abuse of power or occasional infiltration by [[Criminal Organization|Criminal Organizations]] such as [[The Black Legion]], which was revealed during the [[1623 AC Orange Corral Incidents]].

### Public Perception
Public opinion of the Peace Keepers is deeply divided. While many acknowledge their role in civilian protection and conflict stabilization, their neutrality is often interpreted in conflicting ways depending on region and political climate.

In areas influenced by [[Political Corruption]] and [[Propaganda]], especially across different [[Economical Class|economic classes]], the Peace Keepers are sometimes portrayed negatively. Critics in these regions frame them less as neutral protectors and more as external actors interfering in local sovereignty, with their failures and limitations emphasized over their successes.

As a result, their reputation remains inconsistent. Respected in some societies, mistrusted or even vilified in others.
