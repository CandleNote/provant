**The Union of Vallarian Nations** (UVN for short) is an international organization within the [[Continent of Vallarian]], formed at the end of the [[The Great Vallarian War]] with the goal of creating an era of peace and harmony across the continent. A place where nations can discuss matter with one and another before conflicts could occur, alongside to help each other during time of disaster. Although such occurrence are rarely done.

It was led by the victorious nations, [[Mondral]] and [[Kastugar]], with support from [[Delstanvia]], which was still a developing nation at the time. However, [[Tanshara]], a member of the [[The Guardian Alliance]], refused to participate, as they opposed aiding nations that were part of the [[The Sovereign Accord]]. This is believed to stem from Tanshara’s hatred toward the [[Great Southern Empire]], which was collapsing during that period.

### Structure :
UVN is led by the **Union of Vallarian Security Council**, which holds the responsibility of maintaining harmony between Vallarian nations. The council believes that preventing war is essential for the stability and future of the [[Continent of Vallarian]].

The council itself includes representatives from every nation within the [[Continent of Vallarian|Land of Valla]]. However, not all nations hold equal voting power. [[Mondral]] and [[Kastugar]] possess significantly greater influence as the founding nations of the Union.

In addition, the Security Council is not composed solely of nations. It also includes representatives from major continental organizations such as the [[Adventurer's Guild]] and the [[Merchant Guilds]], who serve as observers and advisors, offering input and insight on ongoing matters.
#### Political Influence of the UVN :
The influence that the UVN holds over international political matters is primarily based on [[Soft Power (Politics)]]. Its role is centered on mediation, diplomacy, and maintaining balance rather than direct control.

The UVN does not possess a large standing military force, aside from its [[Peace Keepers (UVN)]]. These forces are typically composed of soldiers or adventurers contributed by member nations, making their loyalty to the UVN sometimes questionable.

### Peace Keepers Force :
The Peace Keepers are a force made up of soldiers sent by member nations to support the UVN in fulfilling its goals. However, not all members are assigned through military command. Some individuals choose to join the [[Peace Keepers (UVN)]] on their own, driven by personal ideals rather than national duty.

These individuals are often leaders or high-ranking members within the Peace Keepers, though this is not always the case. Many of them hold a belief almost similar to that of a [[Wayfarer]], striving to bring about a harmonious future for [[Continent of Vallarian|Valla]] and its people.

There have been incidents involving the Peace Keepers, including cases where wrongdoers or military agents attempted to use the organization to influence their own territories or others through the trust placed in the UVN.
