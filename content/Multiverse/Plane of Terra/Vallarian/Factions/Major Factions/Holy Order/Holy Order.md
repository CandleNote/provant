The Holy Order is a public organisation ruled by the [[Holy Order Family]] alongside [[Holy Order Council]], and serves as one of the most influential religious and military institutions within several regions all across [[Continent of Vallarian|Valla]]. The goal is to protect the [[Continent of Vallarian|Valla]] from other worldly danger such as [[Fiends]], [[Fey]], [[Aberration]], and even [[Celestial]] beings. 
### Goals of the Holy Order
The Holy Order has one main goal, which is protecting the [[Continent of Vallarian|Valla]], and even the [[Plane of Terra]], from otherworldly dangers. These actions could range from drastic measures to simply hunting and killing [[Fiends]]. 

However, because of the logistical difficulties of uniting different groups and nations together so they could focus on this main objective, the [[Holy Order Family]] decided to create the [[Holy Order Council]] to deal with this issue, which led to the creation of their [[Four Procedures of the Holy Order.]]
#### The Creation of the Holy Order
They rise in fame during the [[War Between Valla and Hell]], with the leader of the [[Holy Order Family]], [[Pella Sonne Hangminter]], being cursed by the [[Archdevil Levistus]] during the [[Tale of the Three Siblings]], where she could only have one daughter and no other children. This curse was applied towards her children and grandchildren as well, although she also like her brother gain [[Immortality]].

Because of this, she decided alongside her husband, [[Francis Sonne]], to create a military group that did not have any political or economical goals, instead focusing entirely on dealing with otherworldly threats.

With how the [[Continent of Vallarian]] was united during the [[War Between Valla and Hell]], lady [[Pella Sonne Hangminter|Pella]] decided to take the opportunity to work together and combine the churches of different [[Gods & Goddess]] together under a single organisation to protect the people of [[Continent of Vallarian|Valla]].
