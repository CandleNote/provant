The so called Four Procedures of the [[Holy Order]] is a ruleset that was created by the [[Holy Order Family]] for the [[Holy Order]] and the [[Holy Order Council]] on how to deal with various matters. As the wisdom of [[Pella Sonne Hangminter]]’s [[Immortality]] foresaw, the existence of the [[Holy Order]] is necessary for the long-term survival of [[Continent of Vallarian|Valla]].

**Protection Above All**  
The [[Holy Order]] must always prioritize the safety of the [[Continent of Vallarian|Valla]] and the [[Plane of Terra]] above kingdoms, politics, or religion. No member of the Order is allowed to start wars for territorial gain or involve themselves in succession conflicts unless an otherworldly threat is involved.

**No Forbidden Bargains**  
Members of the [[Holy Order]] are forbidden from making contracts, pacts, or alliances with [[Fiends]], corrupted [[Fey]], [[Aberration]]s, or other dangerous entities, even if such power could be used for good intentions. Possession or usage of forbidden relics is heavily restricted under supervision of the [[Holy Order Council]].

**Unity Before Faith**  
No church or group under the [[Holy Order]] may persecute another recognized religion within the organisation. Followers of different [[Gods & Goddess]] must work together against common threats, and internal holy wars are strictly forbidden.

**Necessary Sacrifice**  
If an otherworldly threat becomes impossible to contain peacefully, the [[Holy Order]] is permitted to use extreme measures to eliminate it. This includes execution of corrupted individuals, destruction of cities overtaken by supernatural influence, sealing dangerous regions, and sacrificing strategic locations if necessary for the survival of [[Continent of Vallarian|Valla]].

### Rule Breakers
Anyone who breaks these rules is usually kicked out, although sometimes the [[Holy Order Council]] prefers to keep it private or, in some cases, apply only a simple slap on the wrist, as a major scandal could cause schism within the [[Holy Order]] itself.

### Controversy 
These rules are quite acceptable for most people, except for the 4th (**Necessary Sacrifice**), where some people are still uneasy with a [[Factions|Faction]] having military power that can make some countries within [[Continent of Vallarian|Valla]] fall to their knees.

Alongside the [[Holy Order]] acting like a ruler over all churches and religious groups within [[Continent of Vallarian|Valla]], which somewhat forces them to follow their rules and procedures, it becomes difficult for them to maintain any freedom in how they would prefer to deal with matters. This sometimes leads to [[Breakaway Factions]] forming in response.
