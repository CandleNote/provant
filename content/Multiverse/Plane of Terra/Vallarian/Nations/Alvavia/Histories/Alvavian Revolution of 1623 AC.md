The Alvavian Revolution of 1623 After Calamity was an event that transformed the government of [[Alvavia]] from a [[Parliamentary Republic]] operating under [[Census Suffrage]] (commonly known as the [[Alvavia|Alvavian Republic]]) into a [[Monarchy]] ruled through [[Autocracy]] by the [[Alsberd Family]]. The family had previously ruled the [[Alvavian Region]] before losing power during [[The Great Vallarian War]].

Their return to power was led by [[Freya Alsberd]], the daughter of the former [[Emperor of Alvavia]], [[Albwin Vel Alsberd]], who had previously been abdicated. Under her leadership, the [[Alsberd Family]] reclaimed control of [[Alvavia]], bringing an end to the republican government and restoring the monarchy.

The population of Alvavia was already largely in favor of the [[Alsberd Family]] compared to the [[Parliament of Alvavia]], which had become increasingly associated with abuses of power. Furthermore, those who could participate in the parliament were rarely members of the [[Lower Class]] or even the [[Middle Class]], instead consisting mostly of established businessmen and former [[Nobility]].

The [[Alvavian Military]] was also strongly supportive of the [[Alsberd Family]]. [[Albwin Vel Alsberd]]'s defeat during [[The Great Vallarian War]] was widely viewed as a failure of [[The Guardian Alliance]] rather than the [[Alvavian Military]] itself. Many historians agree with this assessment, arguing that if the military had not been forced to fight on multiple fronts simultaneously, it may have prevailed in a direct one-on-one conflict.

### Beginning of the Revolution

The catalyst for the revolution was the [[Unknown Death of Albwin Vel Alsberd]] in 1623 After Calamity. His death is widely believed to have been the result of poisoning. Despite already being an elderly [[High Elf]], Albwin was only 605 years old and was expected to live for at least another 150 years.

The incident occurred during a private family gathering where [[Albwin Vel Alsberd]] briefly disappeared. At the time, nobody suspected foul play. However, the following morning he was found dead. By then, he had already been deceased for several hours, placing him beyond the reach of [[Revivify]].

Many believe the assassination was orchestrated by politicians within the [[Parliament of Alvavia]] who feared that the [[Alvavian Military]] might attempt to restore [[Albwin Vel Alsberd]] to power. This fear had grown due to stagnant military wages and the loss of honor and prestige suffered by the armed forces after the war.

### Rise of Freya Alsberd
The assassination had the opposite effect of what its perpetrators intended.

At the time, the [[Alsberd Family]] had only one direct heir remaining within Alvavia: [[Freya Alsberd]]. Her younger brother, [[Eberhard Alsberd]], had sided with [[Delstanvia]] during [[The Great Vallarian War]] and would later become the [[King of Delstainvia]].

Seizing the opportunity, [[Freya Alsberd]] raised her banner in rebellion with the support of the [[Alvavian Military]], sympathetic politicians, and members of the [[Nobility]] who remained loyal to the [[Alsberd Family]].

### The Civil War
The war itself was remarkably swift, lasting only two months.

The popularity of the [[Alsberd Family]] throughout most cities meant that resistance was limited. Only two major cities openly rejected the revolution: [[Aurum]], the capital of the [[Alvavia|Alvavian Republic]], and the [[City of Southguard]].

This divided the conflict into two separate campaigns.

[[Freya Alsberd]] personally led the Aurum Campaign, transporting her followers aboard [[Arcane Airships]] toward the capital. Meanwhile, two young generals, [[Arno Hengsbach]] and [[Mike Breitner]], commanded separate armies in her name.

Victory in the Aurum Campaign came quickly. Support for the [[Parliament of Alvavia]] within the city was weak, and many weapon stockpiles had already been secured by supporters of the [[Alsberd Family]] before the fighting began.

The [[City of Southguard]] Campaign proved to be a longer struggle. [[Freya Alsberd]] specifically ordered that the city and its surrounding lands suffer as little damage as possible. As a result, Generals [[Arno Hengsbach]] and [[Mike Breitner]] conducted a cautious campaign aimed at minimizing destruction rather than achieving a rapid victory.

The conflict accelerated when [[Freya Alsberd]] personally arrived with reinforcements. There she demonstrated that she was not merely a political figurehead. Like her father before her, she proved herself to be an exceptional military commander, personally leading her forces and ultimately securing victory for the revolution.

With the fall of Southguard, the [[Parliament of Alvavia]] collapsed, and the [[Alsberd Family]] once again assumed control of [[Alvavia]], restoring the monarchy after decades of republican rule.
