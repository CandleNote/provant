The Alsberd Family is a noble house of [[High Elf|High Elves]] that originally served as a major noble family within the [[City of Everspring]]. They were later assigned to govern the [[Alvavian Region]] due to their military skill and their powerful bloodline, which is closely connected to the [[Fey]].

The family proved themselves during the [[Colonisation of the Alvavian Region]], following the [[Alvavian Expedition]] funded by [[Magusvia]]. With Magusvia’s support, they established the [[Alvavia|Alvavian Empire]] and became its ruling dynasty, taking the title of [[Emperor of Alvavia]].
### History
#### Emperor Emanuel Alsberd
The first member of the family sent to rule Alvavia was [[Emanuel Alsberd]]. He established the early imperial structure and secured Alsberd authority over the region.

In his later years, he peacefully retired due to old age, passing the throne to his son.

#### Emperor Albwin Vel Alsberd
[[Albwin Vel Alsberd]] succeeded Emanuel as the next [[Emperor of Alvavia]]. His reign continued the Alsberd imperial legacy.

However, during the [[Abducaction of the Alsberd]], he was forced to abdicate to prevent a civil war within [[Alvavia]]. This event led to the rise of the [[Parliament of Alvavia]] and the establishment of republican rule.  After abdication, Albwin retired to a private residence in the [[City of Halverbern]].

#### Eberhard Alsberd and Delstanvia
Albwin’s younger child, [[Eberhard Alsberd]], took a different path during [[The Great Vallarian War]].

He joined the [[Delstainvia Revolution Front]], which ultimately won the war and formed the nation of [[Delstanvia]]. Following the victory, Eberhard was chosen by the people to become the [[King of Delstainvia]].

Eberhard was known for his charisma, Fey-connected bloodline, and military capability. However, most historians agree he was not as strategically skilled as his sister, [[Freya Alsberd]]. The Alsberd Family remains one of the most influential dynasties in the region, shaping the political fate of both [[Alvavia]] and [[Delstanvia]].

#### Unknown Death Incident
Albwin’s life ended during the [[Unknown Death of Albwin Vel Alsberd]] incident, where he was believed to have been poisoned.

Despite being a [[High Elf]] of advanced age (605 years old), he was still expected to live for approximately 150 more years. His death became the catalyst for the [[Alvavian Revolution of 1623 AC]].

####  Freya Alsberd and the Revolution
Following Albwin’s death, his eldest child, [[Freya Alsberd]], led the restoration of the Alsberd rule.

She ultimately became the central figure in reclaiming power and was later recognized as [[Emperor of Alvavia]] after the revolution succeeded.
