The city of Lightfield, also known as The Dock City of Eagle Coast, is a major trading port that allows [[Alvavia]] to trade with other countries through the [[Sea of Calamity]] trade route. Even if the city of Lightfield is not the most important trade city, it still holds a major point of interest for many people thanks to its beautiful beaches and lovely sky.

The merchants of Lightfield have a major influence in the city due to their economic power, which led to the creation of the [[Coucil of Noble of Lightfield]]. This council has stood for a long time and was originally formed by a group of major merchant families who decided to work together to grow their wealth, but it has slowly shifted into a governing system.

Even with the economic strength that the city of Lightfield holds, it is not one of the richest cities within the northern section of Alvavia, as Lightfield does not possess as many resources or materials as other territories.

### Government :
The government of the city of Lightfield has been ever-shifting, as the city has experienced many changes due to the long history of the once small settlement that existed before it became a city during the era of the [[War Between Valla and Hell]], where the [[Council of Noble of Lightfield]] first rose.
#### The War Between Valla & Hell Era
Originally, the city was ruled by the [[Reimold Family (Lightfield)]] during the early era of the [[War Between Valla and Hell]]. However, it was later replaced by the [[Council of Noble of Lightfield]] during [[The Schism of Scales Fire]], a civil war between merchant factions due to taxation in the middle of the [[War Between Valla and Hell]]. The conflict eventually ended with the help of adventurers and the people, leading to the council’s creation after the head of the Reimold family made a pact with a devil. This marked the end of the conflict and led to the city’s freedom from hell’s influence.

Because of this, the city of Lightfield became a base of operations for Vallarian heroes and fighters against hell. Even so, the government of Lightfield is still influenced by followers of demonic arts, who usually lurk in the shadows. They have resurfaced several times due to [[The Schism of Scales Fire]] leaving behind many ancient relics of hellish origin, which some [[Fiends]] use for their own gain.
#### Alvavian Empire till Alvavian States Era
Not much changes during the combination of the City of Lightfield who was close ties with the [[Alvavian Expedition]] and the [[Colonisation of the Alvavian Region]] as the City of Lightfield is already interconnected with trade with [[Magusvia]] anyway.

Even during the Alvavian States Era, the [[Council of Noble of Lightfield]] stay strong as they possess many political powers alongside economical power as well. This is the reason the [[Merchant Guilds]] first expansion after establishing themself in [[Aurum]] to be in the City of Lightfield.
#### 2nd Alvavian Empire
Even after the [[Alvavian Revolution of 1623 AC]], and the [[Alsberd Family]] coming back to become the [[Emperor of Alvavia]]. the City of Lightfield doesn't have much chages as the [[Council of Noble of Lightfield]] stay working together and making sure that every family that comes in and stay in will keep the balance of power and will not bring forward instability like what happen during the [[The Schism of Scales Fire]], which still stay strong in most of the [[Nobility]] mind as they are [[Long Life Species]]

### Society :
The society within the city of Lightfield is mostly based on what its people need for survival, with the main focus usually being fishing and trade due to Lightfield’s strong connection to the sea. This is also reflected in how most people in the city have jobs related to fishing or trade, such as fishermen or merchants.
#### Demographics :
Within the city of Lightfield, the racial/species demographics mostly consist of [[Humans]], [[Half-Elves]], and [[Elf|Elves]], primarily [[High Elf]] and [[Sea Elf]]. Even so, other races do exist within the city due to its nature as a trading hub, although their overall numbers are relatively small compared to the major groups.
#### Religion :
The city of Lightfield mostly follow [[Gidro Bal, The God of Trades, Protection, and Craft (NG)]], and [[Kaggri The God of Sailing, Knowledge, and Undead (NN)]] due to their economical relations with the [[Divine Epithet]] of these gods.

### Factions within Lightfield :
In the early history of Lightfield, there were not many factions aside from the establishment of the [[Council of Noble of Lightfield]]. This later led to the invitation of the [[Merchant Guilds]] into Lightfield during the middle of the [[War Between Valla and Hell]]. During that period of Vallarian history, the countries and cities across the continent were far more united—a level of unity not seen again until the [[After Calamity (AC)]] period.

The [[After Calamity (AC)]] brings forward a new era of connection between places, which allows other [[Guilds within Valla]] to enter the City of Lightfield, although their influence is far more limited than the [[Merchant Guilds]] which already set their root deep in the city.

### Geography :
Lightfield is located on the coast of the Eagle Coast within the [[Sea of Calamity]], with it being the northernmost major city in [[Alvavia]]. Even so, it is still connected to the major roads that exist in the [[Alvavia]] road network, thanks to the small corridor of plain ground called the [[Bavarian Corridor]] that exists between the [[Himenkerg Mountains]] and the [[Eagle Coast]].

Lightfield itself does not have many materials within its lands, aside from a few non-magical ores and resources that are mined from the nearby hills. However, due to its coastal location, it possesses an abundance of water-based resources, many of which have been cultivated by the Sea [[Elf|Elves]]. In addition to this, Lightfield also has relatively fertile soil, further supporting its sustainability.
#### Weather:
Lightfield has warm and pleasant weather throughout the year, shaped by the nearby sea. Summer is bright and dry, with long sunny days and a steady ocean breeze that keeps the heat comfortable. 

Autumn brings cooler air and softer sunlight, with more clouds and the occasional rain coming in from the sea. Winter is mild, never too cold, with gentle winds and light rain instead of harsh storms. Spring is fresh and clear, with warming temperatures, calm seas, and bright skies returning after winter.
