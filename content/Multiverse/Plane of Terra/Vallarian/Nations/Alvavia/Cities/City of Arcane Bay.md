The city of Arcane Bay is a city that was founded during the [[Alvavian Expedition]], though it was originally a small settlement. It later discovered a large amount of [[Arcane Crystal]] near its mountains, which caused the town to rapidly expand in both population and business.

As a trading hub for [[Arcane Crystal]] and for the [[Magic Items]] and goods produced from its abundance, Arcane Bay quickly grew in importance. However, because of this rapid growth, the city developed a somewhat unusual culture. It still follows the [[Alvavian Culture Group]], but with a more competitive edge. Where people have a real chance to strike it rich and rise into nobility.

Compared to most of the [[Alvavian Region]], such opportunities exist elsewhere, but they are far more difficult to achieve than in the city of Arcane Bay.

### Society :
The City of Arcane Bay has a strange and distinct culture compared to most of the [[Alvavian Region]]. It is shaped by the clash between opportunity-seeking and exploitation within the city and its surrounding regions due to its massive economic boom, where many people see the City of Arcane Bay as a land of opportunity.

Although due to this, many people fill with different ambition coming in, the culture of the City is transforming. With some higher class people still holding on to the [[Elven Conservative Nobility Culture]] with the demographic of the [[Nobility]] being [[High Elf]].
#### Demographics :
Within the City of Arcane Bay, the upper class is usually dominated by [[High Elf|High Elves]] and [[Wood Elf|Wood Elves]], who have ruled over the region for generations and own most of the industries and land that the city possesses. Although the demographics of the city have shifted towards other races as well, especially within the working class, these positions are largely filled by other [[Humanoid]]s rather than the favored [[Human]]s that the [[Elven Conservative Nobility Culture]] is willing to tolerate.

### Government :
The politics of the city of Arcane Bay is ever changing, as the city have shifted between different governmental system that rule over [[Alvavia]], alongside the major shift of economical goods, and culture through out time. Although the [[Elven Conservative Nobility Culture]] is quite strong where the they rule over the city of Arcane Bay quite strongly. May it be direct or indirect.
#### The Alvavian Empire Era
During this time period, the city of Arcane Bay still followed the [[Nobility]] system, as the country of [[Alvavia]] operated under a [[Feudalism]]-based form of government. Although the system functioned well enough for centuries, it was not without its flaws, as the vast majority of the nobility were [[Elf|Elves]].

Alongside this, during this era, the City of Arcane Bay was not a major city at all, being little more than a small trading port settlement.

#### The Alvavian Republic/Alvavian States Era
After [[The Great Vallarian War]], the [[Alvavia|Alvavian Empire]] became the Alvavian Republic. However, like many other cities during this era, even though democracy was implemented after the [[Abducaction of the Alsberd]], the nobility of Arcane Bay did not change their ways. They continued to rule the settlement, with [[Political Corruption]] helping them maintain their position and influence even if they are no longer 'Lord'.

Even so, the discovery of a large abundance of [[Arcane Crystal]] nearby allowed some other [[Races & Species|Race/Species]] to become part of the noble class. Alongside this, the weakening influence of the [[Alvavian Culture Group]] within the nobility caused a degree of leniency towards such matters. However, the [[Elven Conservative Nobility Culture]] strongly opposed this becoming a precedent.

#### The 2nd Alvavian Empire
After the [[Alvavian Revolution of 1623 AC]], the [[Alsberd Family]] returned to the throne as the [[Emperor of Alvavia]]. This caused a rise of nationalism throughout [[Alvavia]], including within the city of Arcane Bay, as its abundance of [[Arcane Crystal]] became increasingly valuable to the [[Alvavian Military]], which was heavily influenced by the [[Elven Conservative Nobility Culture]].

As a result, Arcane Bay began receiving greater attention from the imperial government, both for its strategic importance and its contribution to the empire's military ambitions.
