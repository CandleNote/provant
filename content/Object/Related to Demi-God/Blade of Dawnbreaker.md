The Blade of Dawnbreaker is the blade that is use by [[Apollyon, The Guardian of Flame]] which have the ability of burning someone in a massive explosion when slash or piece with this blade. made of flame, as the Guardian of Flame wish to bring forward flame of destruction towards everything. 

According to what was describe as well in old drawing/painting it seem the blade was not fully a blade. But it seem to only be the hilt which have a blade made out of flame which come out whenever the the Guardian wish to use it during combat. This made the weapon unique, and some [[Forge Master of Titanfall]] have tried to copy these style of weapon which are called [[Sun Blade]] which then spread all across the multivere of the desaign and sometime the people who made it doesn't realize it's connection.

Even so this Blade of Dawnbreaker was destroyed into multiple pieces when [[Apollyon, The Guardian of Flame]] was fighting [[Euphony, The Guardian of Melody]] during the [[Second Titanfall Trials]] which became the [[Seed of Dawnbreaker]]. The clash between these two [[Guardians of Titanfall]] was so strong as these two were the higher level Guardian that exist in Titanfall, with their clash being one of the main clash that occurred in the land of [[Isle of Cinder]], where the [[Apollyon, The Guardian of Flame]] have taken over the land from the grasp of [[Euphony, The Guardian of Melody]] and the other major guardian of the [[Isle of Cinder]].
