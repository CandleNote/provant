**Lysander Vérité** is a renowned painter from **[[City of Goldencaster]]**, known for his ability to create lifelike paintings of the environments around him. This talent appears to be entirely natural, something he was born with rather than trained into.

Although he is the illegitimate child of the noble **Vérité** family, he is considered an outcast.
He once had an older sister who died in an accident in **1619** [[After Calamity (AC)]]. His mother is unknown, she was neither a noble nor ever found those who searched for her. His father is [[Chevalier Vérité]], the current head of the [[Vérité noble family]].

For those who know the man, Lysander is not a very noble like man, and very much prefer to acted like non-nobility.
