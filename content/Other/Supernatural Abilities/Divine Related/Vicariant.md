**Vicariant** is a term used for individuals who are blessed with some form of magical gift directly by a [[Gods & Goddess]] or [[Demi-Gods]]. This does not mean that these individuals chose to become a Vicariant in the first place. Some accept their fate, while others do not. However, the term remains the same. Those who walk this path are often powerful enough to alter the fate and trajectory of the Multiverse itself.

#### Common Vicariant Candidates :
Usually, [[Wayfarer]] who become Vicariants are individuals who possess the potential to bring about significant change. While the number of Vicariants in the past was already small, after the [[Calamity Era]] and the creation of the [[Pact of Lord Death]], their numbers have decreased even further, as many [[Gods & Goddess]] no longer grant their power unless it is absolutely necessary.

However, the number of [[Demi-Gods]] who grant their power to [[Wayfarer]] has increased, as the ongoing rivalry between these [[Demi-Gods]] continues to escalate. Opposing [[Demi-Gods]] compete for dominance over their own [[Divine Epithet]] or simply act out of their own ego.

#### Goal of Vicariant :
Vicariants do not always choose this path. Some are forcefully granted this power because their patron may not even realize they have bestowed it, may not care about the Vicariant’s opinion, may be unable to communicate with them, or for many other reasons.

Because of this, defining a singular goal for Vicariants is difficult. Each Vicariant has their own desires, which may align with their patron’s will but not always, especially for those who are forced into this role. Some even attempt to resist or oppose their patron. Being a Vicariant does not only grant power, it often comes with a cost that affects the mind or body of the individual.

#### Effect of Gifted to Vicariant :
The effects of being gifted vary for every Vicariant. Generally, it grants empowerment along with a drawback that reflects the theme or element of the patron’s [[Divine Epithet]].

The power granted to a Vicariant is influenced not only by their patron, but also by the Vicariant’s own prior capabilities. These abilities are amplified far beyond their natural limits, allowing them to achieve feats that would otherwise take a lifetime or be impossible altogether. This enables them to fulfill their own desires and or those of their patron.

However, such gifts can sometimes be more of a curse in disguise. Some patrons grant power that is too overwhelming for a mortal body or mind to handle, especially for those who are untrained. In certain cases, a patron may unknowingly grant power, causing the Vicariant to draw upon an unstable or excessive amount of [[Magical Energy]], enough to potentially destroy them completely.
