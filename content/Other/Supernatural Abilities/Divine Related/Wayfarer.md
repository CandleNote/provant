**Wayfarer** is a term used for individuals who follow a [[Gods & Goddess]] or a philosophical belief with enough conviction to dedicate their ambition and path to it. This belief grants them strength, sometimes manifesting as [[Divine Magical Energy]], a form of magic created through belief or by being believed in.

Not all Wayfarers show visible power, as some only gain stronger resolve or presence, while others can manifest it into magic. The strength of a Wayfarer depends on how firm their belief is, as doubt can weaken their path, which could be manifest itself super human strength. 
Even so there are some chosen few that are empower by their believe directly making them [[Vicariant]] which is a term use for a Wayfarer that is blessed directly by their deity, allowing them to access their deity's power.

The existence and connection of Wayfarer with super humanoid power is another pillar to [[The Ambition Theory]] as most people are Wayfarer to a path that they believe upon without noticing it most of the time, because it self is innate for every thinking creature according to some follower of [[Six Stars of Fate]].
### Different Type of Wayfarer :
There are many type of wayfarers that exist in the multiverse, some [[Scholars of Expression]] try to note down and difference to understand the psyche of consciousness creature.
#### The Divine Wayfarer :
Divine Wayfarers anchor their belief in a higher entity such as a [[Demi-Gods]] or a [[Gods & Goddess]]. Through this connection, they are granted blessings that allow them to access [[Divine Magical Energy]] more directly.
#### The Aversion Wayfarer :
When a Wayfarer’s belief breaks, it does not always fade but can instead change. Aversion Wayfarers are those whose belief has turned into hatred or rejection of what they once followed. Their power becomes unstable and altered, often reflecting their inner conflict. 
Most are evil, but it doesn't always lead in such direction. Alongside some doesn't even notice their aversion from their original path.
#### The Hollow Wayfarer :
Hollow Wayfarers are those who have lost their belief but still retain fragments of their power. Their strength/power is ussually far weaker, unstable, or fading, often leaving them incomplete. Although these people can regain their flame if their ambition is regain.
#### The Ideal Wayfarer :
Ideal Wayfarers follow a philosophical belief rather than a [[Gods & Goddess]]. Their power comes purely from their own conviction upon that believe, allowing them to shape a strange [[Divine Magical Energy]] without relying on a higher being. 
As the magic itself doesn't rely on always using power for a divinity, but instead from just the existence of strong believe which believe to relate to the [[Imaginatio]] or [[Memoria]].
