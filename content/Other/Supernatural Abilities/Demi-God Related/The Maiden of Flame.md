**The Maiden of Flame** is a term used for individuals who are blessed, directly or indirectly, by [[Apollyon, The Guardian of Flame]]. Unlike other [[Wayfarer]]s of [[Apollyon, The Guardian of Flame|Apollyon]], these Maidens carry a [[Seed of Dawnbreaker]] within them, allowing them to access a portion of the Guardian of Flame’s power, capable of reducing almost anything to ash.

A Maiden of Flame is not always a believer or follower of the Guardian. Some are forced into this state, while others become one through accident. Not everyone can bear the power of the Guardian of Flame; only those who can attune to the [[Seed of Dawnbreaker]] are capable. This often means they carry within their heart a desire, impulse, or lingering wish for destruction.

#### Side Effect for Maidens :
The side effects of becoming a Maiden of Flame can be severe. One of the more common outcomes is the gradual loss of sight, especially among those who are highly attuned to their [[Seed of Dawnbreaker]] and frequently draw upon its power. This is often considered the most merciful fate.

In worse cases, a Maiden may lose their physical body or even their mind. Those who suffer a mental collapse are often referred to as broken Maidens of Flame, and they usually emerge from individuals who deny or refuse to accept their power.
