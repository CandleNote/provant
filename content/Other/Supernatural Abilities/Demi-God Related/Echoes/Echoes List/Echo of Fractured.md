Whenever you activate the **Echo of Fractured** as a magic action, you summon another version of yourself. The summon appears in an unoccupied space you can see within 30 feet. You can only have one summon at a time.

The summon has the same Strength score as you at the moment it is created. It also gains all resistance types granted by your species, class, or feats, even if those features are not currently active.
#### Hit Point Scale :
The summon’s hit points are equal to the amount of your own hit points you choose to spend when creating it. This feature cannot reduce you below 1 hit point. The summon cannot be healed and cannot regenerate hit points by any means, except through the spell _wish_.
#### Dismissing :
You can dismiss the summon at no cost, although this action can only be done at the end of yours or other creature's turn. However, you can only do so once, and you must wait until the start of your next turn before doing so again.

When the summon is dismissed, you regain hit points equal to the summon’s current hit points. This healing is considered a special type of healing that ignores resistance, immunity, and healing reduction. The only effect that can prevent this healing is a feature with **Echo Block**.

Whenever you're drop to 0 hit points, your summon would instantly be dismiss, and activate it's healing dismiss feature.
#### Summon's Action :
The summon can move and make one melee weapon attack as part of its action on your turn.

Whenever you take the Attack action, you can forgo one of your attacks to allow the summon to make an additional melee weapon attack. There is no limit to how many times you can do this, as long as you have attacks to give up.

The weapon would take it's binding weapon form. This weapon would deal damage of 2d4+Strength modifier piercing/slashing/bludgeoning/force damage. Which you can choose every time you make an attack with your summon
#### Binding Summon :
You can bind the summon into a simple or martial weapon without any cost. While bound, you may choose to summon it at the start of battle.
