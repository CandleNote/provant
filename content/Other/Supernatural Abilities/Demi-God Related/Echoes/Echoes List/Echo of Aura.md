You can summon **1 Infinite Aura Tower per turn**, and you can have up to **3 towers at the same time**. Each tower lasts for **1 minute**, and each one keeps track of its own time.

You can place a tower in an empty space you can see within **60 feet**.  you can remove any of your towers on your turn without using an action.
### **Cost**
When you summon a tower, you take damage equal to **3 × your proficiency bonus**. This damage **cannot be reduced in any way**.

Your **maximum hit points are also reduced** by an amount equal to your proficiency bonus.
### **Untargetable**
Enemies cannot target the towers.  
Creatures can move through them because they count as objects.
### **Lock-In**
Any creature within **60 feet** of a tower gains **Lock-In**.
For each stack of **Lock-In**, the creature gains **10 temporary maximum hit points**.
### **Tower Attack**
Each tower acts right after your turn.
On its turn, the tower makes **one ranged spell attack** like _fire bolt_ (as a 1st-level effect).
- It uses your spell attack bonus
- It does **not** gain any bonus from **Lock-In**
- You can choose the damage type: **Acid, Cold, Fire, Lightning, or Thunder**
