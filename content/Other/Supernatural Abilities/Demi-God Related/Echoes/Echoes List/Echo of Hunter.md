Whenever you activate **Echo of Hunter**, you summon multiple duplicates of yourself, each wielding a simple or martial ranged weapon that you selected during your last long rest.

These duplicates can appear anywhere within 90 feet of you, as long as you can see the space. Each duplicate immediately makes one ranged weapon attack against a target it can see. Each attack uses its own separate attack roll and damage roll. After they make an attack they would disappear 
#### Summoning Forward :
The number of duplicates you summon is determined by how much damage you are willing to take.

For each duplicate, you take maximum hit reduction equal to your proficiency bonus. After determining the total number of duplicates, you take additional damage equal to the number of duplicates summoned. This damage cannot be reduced in any way.

You cannot summon more duplicates than 3/4 your current level (rounded down).
#### Vendetta of the Hunt :
Each target hit by a duplicate gains one stack of **Vendetta of the Hunt**, which lasts until the creature completes a short or long rest.

During your turn, you can choose a creature affected by **Vendetta of the Hunt** and consume all of its stacks to activate one of the following effects: **Banquet**, **Reprisal**, or **Hush**.

There is no limit to how many times you can use these effects. However, you must be able to perform either a verbal command or a somatic component. You can do this as long as two of your hands or two of your feet are free. This requirement is still considered fulfilled even if you are wielding a two-handed weapon.
### **Banquet**
You consume all stacks of **Vendetta of the Hunt** on a single target.

You regain hit points equal to the number of stacks consumed multiplied by your proficiency bonus. This healing is considered a special type of healing that ignores resistance, immunity, and healing reduction. The only effect that can prevent this healing is a feature with **Echo Block**.
### **Reprisal**
You consume all stacks of **Vendetta of the Hunt** from any number of creatures you can see at the time of activation.

Your maximum hit points increase by 15 for each stack consumed, and you heal for the same amount. You also gain the effect **O' Hear My Vow Fellow Hunter**, which cannot stack and lasts until the end of your next turn.

Before this effect begins, record your current hit points. At the end of your next turn, if your current hit points exceed the recorded value, they are reduced to match the recorded value.
### **Hush**
You consume all stacks of **Vendetta of the Hunt** within 300 feet of you. You do not need to see the affected creatures.

For every three stacks consumed, you gain one additional free ranged weapon attack using a simple or martial weapon of your choice.

After using **Hush**, you cannot use it again until the start of your next turn.
