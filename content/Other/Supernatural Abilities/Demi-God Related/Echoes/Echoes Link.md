Echoes Link (or simply **Echoes**) is a power formed from the remnants of the [[Eldar]] after their defeat by [[Vallarian]]. These powers were later created by the [[First Sultan of Prospero]] during the [[First War of Prosperian Unification]].

He created it from the [[Eldar]] power and spread it by handing it to his trusted men and nobles, which split these Echoes into 496 (not including [[The Echo of Season]]). Because of this, the pure [[Magical Energy]] was able to cultivate each Echo into its own unique form.

However, the war ended with the betrayal of the [[First Sultan of Prospero]] by his right hand man, who was also his childhood friend. This also marked both the start and the end of the Echo Cycle.

### The Night of Knife in Echoes
After the war, [[The Night of Knife in Echoes]] took place. Many [[Echoes User]] loyal to the Sultan were killed due to a suspected insurrection. According to tales, the Sultan killed another of his childhood friends, believing she was the leader of the rebellion.

In the end, the [[First Sultan of Prospero]] was defeated by the [[Valk Family]], [[Barlett Family]], [[The Najjar Family]], [[The Naad Family]], and [[الصياد (alsayaad/The Hunter)]], who was also his childhood friend.

### The Echo Cycle
At the moment of his death, the Sultan used the last of his Echoes Link, [[The Echo of Season]], to create an endless cycle. When an [[Echoes User]] dies, their Echo remains in Prospero. This is known as the **Echo Cycle**.

This act split [[The Echo of Season]] into four pieces, each becoming one of the most powerful Echoes. A random noble and a random commoner are granted Echoes in rotation whenever an [[Echoes User]] dies.

It is believed that this so-called curse was placed upon the world so the [[First Sultan of Prospero]] could return one day. He believed that people would become selfish and greedy without the correct ruler, and that this would eventually prove him right.

### Echo Stones
After [[الصياد (alsayaad/The Hunter)]] found a way to manage this by turning Echoes within a person into **Echo Stone**, the curse of the [[First Sultan of Prospero]] still remained. Echo Stones cannot be destroyed by any mortal.

Some heroes are believed to have gained Echoes through Echo Stones.
