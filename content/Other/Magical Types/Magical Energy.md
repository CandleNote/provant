**Magical Energy** is the term used to describe the supernatural power that exists within every creature possessing a [[Soul]], as well as certain objects known as [[Magic Items]]. This energy allows its wielder to perform extraordinary feats and manifest abilities capable of altering both themselves and their surroundings.
#### The Difference of Magical Energy
There are four types of Magical Energy that exist within the multiverse:
- [[Primordial Magical Energy]] : The magical energy that has existed since the [[Start of the Universe]] and appears to be the purest form of Magical Energy.
- [[Arcane Magical Energy]] : Based upon harnessing the energy within oneself efficiently enough to conjure spells.
- [[Druidic Magic]] : Based upon harnessing the energy generated by plants, animals, and other living beings.
- [[Divine Magical Energy]] : Based upon believing in something with great devotion or being believed in by others.
#### Expression of Magical Energy
While all beings possess Magical Energy, no two individuals are exactly alike. The nature, potency, and characteristics of one's Magical Energy are often influenced by their personality, experiences, desires, and the state of their [[Soul]]. As a result, two people may wield the same form of magic yet express it in entirely different ways.

#### Will of Ambition & Power Scaling
According to the [[Ambition Theory]], Magical Energy is closely connected to the [[Will of Ambition]] that resides within every [[Soul]]. The stronger an individual's ambitions, convictions, and determination, the more powerful their Magical Energy becomes. This connection also explains why a person's physical and martial capabilities often improve alongside their magical growth, as both draw strength from the same source.

#### Regeneration
Magical Energy naturally regenerates over time, though the rate differs between individuals. Excessive use may result in exhaustion, weakened abilities, or even damage to the [[Soul]] itself if pushed beyond safe limits.

The rate of regeneration appears to depend on both the condition of an individual's body and their efficiency in utilizing Magical Energy. Those with healthier bodies or greater mastery over their Magical Energy are often capable of recovering it more quickly than others.
