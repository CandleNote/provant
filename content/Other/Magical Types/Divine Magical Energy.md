Divine Magical Energy is a type of [[Magical Energy]] that differs from other forms of magical energy, as it originates from belief itself or from being believed in by other [[Soul|Souls]]. The power of belief is a potent force that has caused many historical figures to rise or fall throughout history. Even [[Gods & Goddess]] and [[Demi-Gods]] rely upon this power, as it amplifies their existing [[Magical Energy]].

#### Requirement of Divine Magical Energy
##### Believer Side
The strange aspect of Divine Magical Energy from the believer's side is that they do not need to believe in a being such as a [[Gods & Goddess]] or [[Demi-Gods]]. They simply need to believe in something. As long as it is something they truly believe in, they are capable of generating Divine Magical Energy, which is why Divine Magical Energy overlaps with the strange realm of [[Imaginatio]].

However, this also means that a believer does not need to believe in something noble or graceful. Throughout history, there have been many individuals fueled by intense hatred toward something, or by twisted beliefs such as self-ego and self-worship, who were able to manifest Divine Magical Energy through the strength of their conviction alone. Such individuals are often categorized as Aversion or Hollow [[Wayfarer]].

##### The Patron
While those who are believed in by [[Mortals]] also gain Divine [[Magical Energy]], the amount gained appears to be less than that generated by the believer themselves. However, when large numbers of people place their faith in a single being, it can become a significant source of empowerment for these creatures, who are often already powerful to begin with.

It seems that Patrons also prefer those who are stronger in the [[Will of Ambition]] department, which further supports the [[Ambition Theory]]. These stronger individuals are usually more devoted to the ideals of their Patron. Furthermore, it appears that the stronger a person's [[Will of Ambition]] is, the more Divine Magical Energy they produce.
