[[Fey]] are not a singular species, but rather a broad classification of beings that differ greatly in form, power, and behavior. Their type is often defined by their connection to emotion, concept, or role within the [[Feywild]].

##### **Archfey :**
Archfey are among the most powerful [[Fey]], often ruling over domains within the [[Feywild]]. These beings embody powerful concepts, emotions, or stories, and their presence alone can reshape reality around them.

Their connection to [[Imaginatio]] is extremely strong, allowing them to alter environments, enforce contracts, and influence large regions. Many Archfey form [[Fey Courts]] or followings, and their power may rival that of [[Demi-Gods]] or even themselves are [[Demi-Gods]] level.

Alongside their mastery doesn't just limit them to [[Imaginatio]], but [[Druidic Magic]] as their connections to the feywild allow them to tap to the natural magic of the world that they exist in, also [[Divine Magical Energy]] as well sometime as their ideals and believe can empower them beyond any normal [[Fey]].

##### **Lesser Fey :**
Lesser [[Fey]] are the most commonly encountered type of [[Fey]]. These include beings such as sprites, pixies, satyrs, and similar entities.

While individually weaker than Archfey, they are often more numerous and unpredictable. They tend to act based on curiosity, emotion, or mischief, and frequently interact with mortals through small-scale bargains or tricks.

##### **Court Fey :**
Court [[Fey]] are those aligned with structured groups within the [[Feywild]], often serving under powerful Archfey or within organized courts.

Their behavior is more defined by rules, hierarchy, and tradition compared to other [[Fey]]. Despite this structure, their logic and values still differ greatly from mortal understanding, and their loyalty is often tied to symbolism rather than strict duty.

##### **Wild Fey :**
Wild [[Fey]] are untamed and often lack allegiance to any court or higher authority. They are closely tied to raw emotion, natural forces, or chaotic expressions of [[Imaginatio]].

These [[Fey]] are highly unpredictable and may act without clear reason or pattern. Their presence often causes drastic changes in their surroundings, especially in areas where reality is already unstable.

##### **Hag-kind :**
Hags are a darker classification of [[Fey]] that embody corruption, decay, or twisted aspects of emotion and desire.

Unlike many other [[Fey]], hags often engage in manipulative bargains with clear harmful intent. They are known for exploiting loopholes, creating long-term suffering, and twisting outcomes in ways that benefit themselves.
