**Constructs** are artificial beings created through magic, engineering, or a combination of both. Unlike most creatures, Constructs are not born, but built or formed for a specific purpose.

Constructs are typically made from materials such as metal, stone, wood, or arcane components. They are animated by enchantments, bound spirits, or complex mechanisms. Most Constructs lack true free will and operate according to the commands of their creator.
### Intelligence and Free Will
Most Constructs have limited intelligence and follow strict instructions. However, some rare Constructs develop independent thought, emotions, or personalities, especially those created with powerful magic or experimental designs or even having with [[Soul]] within it.
### Examples of Constructs
Typical examples of Construct creatures include:
- Golems
- Animated armor
- Mechanical guardians
- Clockwork beings
- Living statues
- Magical automatons
- Warforge
- Autognome

#### Free Will Construct :
Some constructs are granted free will, allowing them to make their own decisions, much like other thinking beings that exist across the multiverse. However, such cases are rare, as creating a construct with its own sense of emotion requires extraordinary conditions.

This form of free will is usually achieved through magical means, often involving a high concentration of [[Arcane Magical Energy]] to sustain an artificial intelligence within the construct. In some cases, a true independent mind can be created through [[Soul]] transference into the construct’s body.

Alternatively, a [[Memetic creature]] or an [[Imaginatio Aberration]] may inhabit a construct body, either knowingly or unknowingly taking control of it. In such instances, the construct’s “free will” does not originate from itself, but from the entity residing within it.
