The Pact of [[Lord Death]] is an agreement created after the [[Calamity Era]] between the [[Gods & Goddess]] within the [[Multiverse]] to not directly interfere with the actions of mortals. Many believe it is connected to the [[Enemy of Creation]], as [[Lord Death]] has a major link to the [[Calamity Event]]. Although, like everything related to that era, not much is known about why or how it was formed. Only that its consequence is that the [[Gods & Goddess]] have chosen not to intervene directly in mortal affairs, as they watch from a distance.
### Goal of the Pact
Some believe that the Pact of [[Lord Death]] exists to allow the [[Will of Ambition]] to become more abundant, which is further supported by the [[Ambition Theory]]. However, the true purpose of the pact remains unknown.
