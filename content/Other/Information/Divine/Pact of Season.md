The Pact of Season is a deal created by the [[Druidic Pantheon]] to ensure peace between one another before the creation of the [[Pact of Lord Death]]. Since the [[Druidic Pantheon]] was already peaceful, the main purpose of the pact was to prevent their [[Wayfarer]] from waging [[Crusade]]s against one another, similar to what was happening on [[Titanfall Isle]] during the [[Titanfall Trial]].

#### History of the Pact of Season
According to legends passed down by most [[Druids]], the Pact of Season was originally proposed by [[Lysithea]], the Goddess of Summer, Joy, and Harvest, to [[Amandri]], the God of Autumn, Misfortune, and Exploration. [[Amandri]] had grown concerned over the rise of a new [[Gods & Goddess|Goddess]] who ruled over a new [[Divine Epithet]] in modern-day [[Everwinter]]. This goddess was [[Vehtya]], the Goddess of Winter, Hunting, and Family. Both [[Lysithea]] and [[Amandri]] were uncertain of where her alignment would stand, fearing that conflict could emerge between the seasonal deities. However, rather than seeking war, they instead wished to preserve peace among the [[Druidic Magic]]-based [[Gods & Goddess]].

Because of this, [[Lysithea]] and [[Amandri]] met with [[Ethaka]], the God of Spring, Fertility, and Loyalty, who agreed with the idea of forming a pact between the seasonal gods. The purpose of the pact was to ensure that neither the gods nor their [[Wayfarer]] would come into conflict with one another, especially in regions where their influences overlapped, as radical [[Druids]] could potentially wage war against each other in the gods’ names.

Eventually, [[Lysithea]], [[Amandri]], and [[Ethaka]] met with the newly ascended [[Vehtya]]. The details of this meeting remain unknown, but the creation of the pact itself is certain. As part of the agreement, four major [[Druidic Grove]]s were established as the [[Everseason Spots]], sacred locations spread across the [[Continent of Vallarian|Valla]].
