**Divine Epithets** are titles used to describe [[Gods & Goddess]] or even lesser beings such as [[Demi-Gods]]. They represent the domains, functions, attributes, or aspects of a divine entity itself, as understood and interpreted by their [[Wayfarer]]. Most of the time, it is the foundation of their understanding in the most simplest way to explain their divine wishes that people latch on towards.

Some [[Demi-Gods]], like the [[Guardians of Titanfall]], are very much influenced by their Divine Epithets, as they are creatures formed from people's beliefs. Although some deities are far removed from their so-called Divine Epithets, these are often based on the personality of the being itself, which usually occurs because the being was once mortal before their ascension to godhood. For some, they retain their humanity, which in turn shapes their Divine Epithets.
