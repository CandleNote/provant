### Origins
Leviathan is a term used for two different types of powerful beings that exist across the multiverse. One of them is the [[Elemental Leviathan]], creatures formed within the [[Primordial Plane of Water]]. Their name comes from the original Leviathan that gave birth to them, known as the [[Primordial Leviathan]], a being believed to have created the [[Primordial Plane of Water]]. It is said to be one of the oldest creatures in existence, with ties to [[Lord Death]].
### Knowledge and Records
Knowledge of Leviathans is extremely limited. Information regarding [[Elemental Leviathan]] is mostly known only among those connected to the [[Elemental Planes]], while knowledge of the [[Primordial Leviathan]] is largely restricted to those tied to the [[Leyline]], the [[Ethereal Planes]], and the [[Astral Sea]].

Even then, most surviving records and research regarding Leviathans originate from the [[Sea of Leviathan]], a region within the [[Plane of Terra]].
### Historical Events
The [[Sea of Leviathan]] became widely known after [[The Lament of the Fallen]] incident, the catastrophe that led to the ascension of [[Apollyon, The Guardian of Flame]]. The region later gained further historical significance during the [[First Titanfall Trials]], specifically in the conflict known as [[The Battle of Flame and Harmony]].

The battle took place in what was once called the [[Sea of Leviathan|Sea of Terra]], where two [[Witches of Titanfall]] blessed by [[Euphony, The Guardian of Melody]] and [[Naamah, The Guardian of Light]] clashed against one of the [[Archon of Guardian|Archons]] serving [[Apollyon, The Guardian of Flame]].
