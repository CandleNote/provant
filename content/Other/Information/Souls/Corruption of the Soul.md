[[Soul]]s can be corrupted through magic that affects the mind, emotions, or identity of a creature. Because the [[Soul]] acts as the metaphysical mind of a being, enchantment and similar magic used by [[Enchanter]]s may also influence the [[Soul]] itself.

However, permanent [[Soul]] corruption is rare. Even beings such as [[Vampires]], [[Succubi]], and [[Monster of the Voids]] are usually incapable of permanently corrupting a [[Soul]] through enchantment alone.

Deep corruption usually occurs when a creature mentally succumbs to fear, manipulation, trauma, obsession, or prolonged emotional instability. Temporary corruption may occur through ordinary enchantment, but lasting corruption often requires the gradual breaking of a creature’s identity and [[Will of Ambition]] over time.

Severe corruption may alter a creature’s [[Memoria]], emotions, personality, or sense of self. In extreme cases, the [[Soul]] may become fractured or unstable.
### Healing a Corrupted Soul
A corrupted [[Soul]] can be healed, though ordinary healing magic is usually insufficient on its own.

Recovery often requires emotional stability, restored [[Memoria]], and a reaffirmed sense of self. Strong bonds, hope, acceptance, and emotional support are common methods of recovery.

Other methods include:
- Breaking curses or enchantments
- Restoring lost memories
- Reaffirming one’s [[Will of Ambition]]
- Removing the source of corruption
- [[Soul]] purification rituals
- [[Divine Magical Energy]]
