Destroying a [[Soul]] is considered one of the greatest [[Cardinal Sin]]s of the multiverse and is rarely attempted. In most known cases, the destruction of a [[Soul]] first requires the [[Soul]] to be captured, commonly through the spell [[Soul Cage]]. However, the knowledge of such magic is exceedingly rare, as it is considered part of the [[Limit of Mortals]] due to being a [[6th Circle Magic]] spell.

If a [[Soul]] is successfully destroyed, it creates a massive tear within the [[Plane of Existence]] where the act was committed, severely destabilizing the surrounding reality.

However, true [[Soul]] destruction is extremely difficult to perform correctly. Most attempts merely release the [[Soul]]’s [[Magical Energy]] in an uncontrolled manner, a phenomenon closely related to the [[Releasing Magical Energy]] problem. While this may fail to fully erase the [[Soul]], it is still considered a [[Cardinal Sin]], as even an incomplete attempt can heavily damage the [[Soul]] and destabilize the multiverse itself.

This uncontrolled release of [[Magical Energy]] often collides directly with the fabric that stabilizes reality, causing fractures within existence and potentially resulting in a [[Damaged Soul|damaged soul]] instead of complete destruction.

In many recorded cases, failed [[Soul]] destruction has resulted in corrupted entities, unstable magical zones, distorted memories, or permanent scars upon the [[Leyline]] itself which take time to heal or never able to.
