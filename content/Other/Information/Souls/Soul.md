Souls are a core component of every living creature, serving as the mind and foundation of the individual. It is within the soul that a creature’s [[Memoria]] (memories), thoughts (Such as [[Imaginatio]]), personality, and way of thinking operate. Which is why the sign of a birth of a new person is by their [[Manifestation of Soul]].

A soul is usually **located within the creature’s brain**, although some beings possess souls elsewhere within their body. However, this remains uncertain, as souls are extremely difficult to [[Tracking a Soul|track]]. The soul is also where a creature’s [[Magical Energy]] is contained. This is why [[Undead]] that still possess souls are often significantly more powerful than those that do not, as the soul contains a being’s beliefs, knowledge, identity, and sense of self.
### Identity
A soul contains the fundamental “data” of an individual that distinguishes them from all other beings. A person’s [[Imaginatio]], [[Memoria]], personality, and [[Will of Ambition]] are all unique, causing every soul to differ from another.

To the [[Leyline]], each soul possesses its own distinct signature, as though it has been indexed and recorded.
### Abilities of the Soul
Souls are the core of a person, serving as the foundation of thought, emotion, and identity. Without a soul, a creature cannot think, feel, or naturally manifest [[Memoria]]. However, this does not mean that something requires a true soul to produce [[Memoria]], as replicated souls are also capable of doing so. In rare cases, these artificial replications may even undergo a [[Manifestation of Soul|Soul Manifestation]] and develop into true souls of their own.

A creature’s [[Magical Energy]] also originates from the soul. This is reflected differently depending on the form of magic being used. For [[Sorcerer]]s, [[Magical Energy]] is often tied to bloodlines and inherited power. For mages, it is shaped through knowledge, understanding, and the refinement of the mind. [[Divine Magical Energy]] manifests through faith, belief, and devotion toward a philosophy, deity, or ideal, whether of oneself or another. Meanwhile, [[Druidic Magic]] is born from the bond between the soul, nature, and surrounding life, allowing such energy to be manifested through harmony and connection.

However, souls also possess a destructive side. If the [[Magical Energy]] within a soul is forcefully released through [[Destruction of the Soul]] or uncontrolled [[Releasing Magical Energy]], it can destabilize both the soul and the surrounding reality itself, often resulting in a [[Damaged Soul]].
### Soul Effect Towards the Laws of the Multiverse
Souls are necessary for most living creatures to survive. The destruction, imprisonment, or permanent corruption of a soul is considered one of the greatest [[Cardinal Sin]]s within the multiverse, often attracting the unwanted attention of [[Lord Death]], as such acts oppose the natural order he seeks to maintain.

However, the existence of souls within a [[Plane of Existence]] not designed to contain them can destabilize the fabric of reality itself. Over time, many souls or simply having a powerful soul, this may create tears within the plane, potentially causing catastrophic ruptures that connect two separate planes together. This is very much can be seen if anyone [[Destruction of the Soul|Destroy a Soul]] as it is one it's consequence.
#### Reincarnation Rule
Some souls possess the ability to reincarnate themselves into a new life, whether once or through endless cycles of rebirth. However, repeated reincarnation places immense strain upon the fabric of reality.

A soul that exists far longer than it was originally meant to may gradually weaken the stability of the universe itself, creating fractures within existence if the cycle continues for too long.

As a result, reincarnation is rarely permitted by the [[Wayfarer]]s of [[Lord Death]].
