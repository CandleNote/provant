[[Soul|Souls]] can be tracked by certain individuals who possess rare gifts. These abilities usually require access to [[Primordial Magical Energy]] or a significant mastery of [[Arcane Magical Energy|Arcane Art]], both of which are exceptionally rare and often only manifest in those with a strong [[Will of Ambition]].

According to surviving records, [[Soul|Souls]] can be differentiated from one another, as each [[Soul]] possesses a unique presence, sensation, or “signature.” Skilled [[Soul]]-trackers are often able to estimate a creature’s power, emotional state, or nature through the strength and quality of its [[Soul]].

Some [[Soul|Souls]] possess a physical form, such as those of [[Wraith]]s and certain other [[Undead]]. These [[Soul|Souls]] are significantly easier to perceive or track. 
### Tracking from Distance
Some [[Diviner]] have the abilities to track someone from a distance through their [[Soul|Souls]]. As the [[Soul|Souls]] are each unique from each other. As each some to have their own identification in the eyes of the [[Leyline]].
### Disguising a Soul
Some creatures possess the rare ability to disguise their [[Soul|Souls]] through magical means. Certain [[Changeling]]s are capable of concealing or altering the signature of their [[Soul|Souls]], making it difficult for others to identify their true nature. However, this level of mastery is uncommon even among [[Changeling]]s.

Some [[Illusionist]]s and [[Transmuter]]s are also capable of imitating these techniques through illusion or transmutation magic, allowing them to distort, conceal, or falsify the appearance of a [[Soul]] in order to deceive [[Soul]]-trackers.
