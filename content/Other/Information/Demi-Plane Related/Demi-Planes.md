A **Demi-Plane** is a small, separate world that exists outside the normal flow of the [[Plane of Existence]]. Unlike full planes such as the [[Plane of Terra]], a Demi-Plane is an artificial space formed through intense magical influence and must remain connected to another plane to exist.

Because a Demi-Plane relies on [[Magical Energy]] to remain stable, it is naturally unstable. If its energy supply weakens or runs out, the Demi-Plane may collapse entirely. Due to this limitation, most Demi-Planes are far smaller than true planes of existence.

According to the [[Demi-Plane Theory]] proposed by the [[First Herrschers]], Demi-Planes are not true worlds, but constructs formed from memory, imagination and magic. Essentially false realities shaped and stabilise by powerful [[Magical Energy]]. 

However, the [[2nd Herrschers of Memory]] later expanded this idea in the [[2nd Demi-Plane Theory]], suggesting that Demi-Planes can grow if sustained by enough [[Memetic Creature]] or [[Imaginatio Aberration]] within them. This was later expanded that perhaps even [[Mortals]] entrap within it is also can help it grow as well.

#### Demi-Plane Life Cycle :
A Demi-Plane follows a unique and unstable life cycle. Most Demi-Planes fail shortly after forming. Without enough [[Magical Energy]], they collapse almost immediately, often releasing a violent surge of magic.

If a Demi-Plane becomes **self-sustaining**, its behavior changes. Instead of collapsing, it begins storing and cycling [[Memoria]] and [[Imaginatio]] within it which is created by creature inhabiting it.

However, a Demi-Plane has a limit.
- If **too little [[Magical Energy]] exists, the Demi-Plane weakens and collapses.
- If **too much [[Magical Energy]] accumulates, the Demi-Plane destabilizes.
- If balanced correctly, the Demi-Plane can persist for a long time.

#### The Resetting of a Demi-Plane :
When a Demi-Plane exceeds its capacity for [[Magical Energy]], it may undergo a **reset**.

During a reset:
- The Demi-Plane collapses inward
- Stored [[Memoria]] or [[Imaginatio]] within the Demi-Plane itself can reshapes the environment. This can also be shaped by the [[Will of Ambition]] of the creature within the Demi-Plane itself.
- The plane reforms as a similar but altered version of itself

This results in a world that looks familiar but has changed details, structures, or history. These changes are influenced by the accumulated memories and emotions of its [[Memetic creature]], or [[Imaginatio Aberration]], or even [[Mortals]] within the Demi-Plane.

If the Demi-Plane successfully absorbs the excess [[Magical Energy]], it stabilizes in its new form.  
If it fails, the Demi-Plane collapses completely and ceases to exist, which causes a magical force blast that is center of where the Demi-Plane is tied to the [[Plane of Existence]] it is on. Which can cause enough damage to destroy a massive area.

#### Interaction With Demi-Planes
Entering a Demi-Plane varies greatly depending on its nature. Some Demi-Planes can be entered easily. While others are completely inaccessible to normal beings. In certain cases, only those known as **[[Memoria Wielder]]** or [[Imaginatio Author]], who can manipulate the fabric of [[Magical Energy]] of the [[Demi-Planes]] itself, are able to perceive or enter them.

- **Distorted Time Perception**  
    Time may flow differently inside a Demi-Plane. Since [[Memoria]] is closely tied to Chrono-based magic, some Demi-Planes can slow, hasten, or loop time within their boundaries.
- **Emotional Echoes**  
    [[Memoria]] and [[Imaginatio]] stored within the Demi-Plane may manifest as emotions, visions, or sensations. In some cases, the Demi-Plane may reflect the visitor’s own memories or emotions back at them.
- **Partial Entry**  
    In rare cases, a Demi-Plane does not pull the physical body inside. Instead, only the consciousness enters, leaving the body behind in reality. This is extremely dangerous, as the body may be left vulnerable to injury, starvation, or death.

Because of these risks, Demi-Planes are considered highly dangerous zones, and interaction with them is strongly discouraged unless one understands their structure or possesses control over [[Magical Energy]] or just simply strong enough to deal with it.
