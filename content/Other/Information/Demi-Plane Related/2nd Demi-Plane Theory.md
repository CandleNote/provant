The **[[Second Demi-Plane Theory]]** was proposed by the [[2nd Herrschers of Memory]] after investigating the [[Memoria Demi-Plane Disaster]] during [[The Great Vallarian War]]. This disaster, which was handled by the [[Shattered Knights]], provided proof for a long-debated idea within the [[Intelligentsia Guild]]: that a **Demi-Plane can become self-sustaining** or worse expanding in their domain.

During this research, the [[2nd Herrschers of Memory]] studied the existence of **[[Memetic creature]]**, first identified by the [[1st Herrschers of Memory]]. These beings are formed entirely from [[Memoria]] and are capable of developing a form of **[[Ego (Self)]]**. However, many of them behave in an instinctive or beast-like manner rather than as fully conscious beings.

This discovery led to a deeper understanding of how [[Demi-Planes]] function.
The theory explains that a Demi-Plane can reach **stability** when the [[Memoria]], or/and [[Imaginatio]] within it creates enough [[Memetic Creature]] or [[Imaginatio Aberration]] to sustain its existence. These creatures, through their thoughts, memories, and actions, continuously generate [[Memoria]]or [[Imaginatio]] which in turn fuels the Demi-Plane itself.

However, this balance is fragile.
If a Demi-Plane is created or given with **too much [[Memoria]]** or [[Imaginatio]], it may expand uncontrollably, as seen in the [[Memoria Demi-Plane Disaster]]. In such cases, the [[Memoria]] forms unstable or artificial personalities, causing rapid growth and distortion of the plane.

If there is **too little [[Memoria]]**, the Demi-Plane cannot sustain itself and will eventually collapse.
According to the theory, all [[Demi-Planes]] eventually reach one of three outcomes:
- **Equilibrium**, where [[Memoria]]/[[Imaginatio]] generation and consumption balance out
- **Collapse**, if [[Memoria]]/[[Imaginatio]] is insufficient
- **Runaway Expansion**, if [[Memoria]]/[[Imaginatio]] is too abundant

This explains why some [[Demi-Planes]] fade quickly, while others grow into dangerous or semi-stable worlds that require outside intervention most of the time, from heavy amount of [[Memoria]] or [[Imaginatio]] creation (Usually from heavy amount [[Emotional Weight]], and [[Will of Ambition]]) or someone tampering with the [[Memoria]]/[[Imaginatio]] like a [[Memoria Wielder]] may it be [[Memory Keeper]] or even [[Memory Burner]] or [[Imaginatio Author]]

#### Notes :
The finding of [[Imaginatio]] was founded later by [[Imaginatio Author]] working together with [[4th Herrschers of Memory]], as the spread of [[Imaginatio]] was founded to be link to the 2nd [[Demi-Plane Theory]] already existing fact.
