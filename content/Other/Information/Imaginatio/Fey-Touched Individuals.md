Some mortals who interact with the [[Fey]] or spend extended time within the [[Feywild]] may become altered by their influence. These individuals, often referred to as [[Fey]]-touched, begin to exhibit traits associated with [[Fey]] nature.

This may include a weakened sense of reality, heightened imagination, emotional instability, or an increased affinity with [[Imaginatio]]. As these people who are attune to the [[Fey]] are the closest people towards the mastery of [[Imaginatio]] most of the time.

In extreme cases, the individual may lose their original identity and become something closer to a [[Fey]] than a mortal, although such occurrences are very rare unless they are already aligned with the [[Fey]] to begin with.

In some cases, this transformation is not caused by the individual’s own connection to [[Imaginatio]], but instead originates from a bargain made with the [[Fey]]. Through such deals, a mortal may be granted a portion of [[Fey]] power in exchange for something of value, binding them further to [[Fey]] influence over time.
