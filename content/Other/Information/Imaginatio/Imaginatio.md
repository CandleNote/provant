**Imaginatio** is a form of power created from the imagination of creatures capable of forming thoughts and ideals. This energy is somewhat similar to [[Memoria]] and [[Divine Magical Energy]], but instead of originating from belief or past experiences, Imaginatio is born from imagination itself.

According to the [[Second Herrschers]], Imaginatio is far more prevalent in individuals who are aligned with the arts or possess highly creative minds, such as children. This is because Imaginatio allows something that seems absurd to manifest into reality. When a person believes strongly enough in their own imagination, it can take form within the real world.

However, this belief does not function in the same way as ideological faith or divine faith. Instead, it is rooted in how the individual perceives reality and fiction. For some, the boundary between the two is thin or even nonexistent. For these individuals, their ability to manifest imagination into reality becomes significantly stronger. Such as [[Fey]] who are naturrally seeming to be talented most of the time with this Imaginatio type magic.
#### Limitation :
A person’s imagination and their belief in their own absurd ideas are the primary factors that determine their ability to access Imaginatio. While some individuals may attempt to believe in their imagination temporarily, their subconscious mind often rejects it. Changing one’s fundamental beliefs is extremely difficult, bordering on impossible, especially when it comes to Imaginatio.

Additionally, the use of Imaginatio is mentally taxing. Those who can access this power tend to be more whimsical in nature, sometimes to the point of appearing unstable. Their perception of reality may already be influenced by their own mental creations rather than the objective world.

This condition can worsen over time. As their imagination grows more powerful, it may spiral out of control, further damaging their mental stability. In extreme cases, individuals may lose their sense of reality entirely and begin to drift toward the [[World of Fiction]].

#### Creation From Imagination :
**Imaginatio** allows fiction to be created into reality, though in a limited manner. The more creative and detached a person is from the boundary between reality and fiction, the more they are able to create. Even so, Imaginatio still requires the user's mental strength and magical capability to function in the first place.

These Imaginatio creations can range from small objects and structures to even [[Imaginatio Aberration]], which are creatures formed from a person's imagination that become real. These creations can also be fueled by [[Memoria]] in addition to Imaginatio, allowing them to become both [[Memetic Creature]] and [[Imaginatio Aberration]] at the same time.

Imaginatio is usually stronger in places with a high concentration of [[Memoria]], as the two are closely connected, both originating from a creature’s psyche and cognition. Imaginatio can weave and manipulate [[Memoria]] in ways that [[Memoria Wielder]] could only dream of.
