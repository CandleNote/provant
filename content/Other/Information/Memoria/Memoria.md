Memoria are strange things that exist in the multiverse which some believe to be connected to the [[Talia the Weaver]], but it self is a supernatural occurrence that happen all around the multiverse where mortals live according to the [[Wayfarer]] of [[Talia the Weaver]]. Memoria does not just mean that it is from the memory of living creature around, but according to the [[Wayfarer]] of [[Talia the Weaver]], it is also fueled by the [[Emotional Weight]] and [[Will of Ambition]] of mortals within a region which form the so called [[Memoria Bubble]] if those properties existed for a long enough time and been produce for a long time. Although the [[Wayfarer]] of [[Talia the Weaver]] have a duty to protect the multiverse from dangerous amount of Memoria creation as most of the time these Memoria could endanger people if was unwatched or use by the wrong person which according to the Legend of [[Shattered Knights]] have occurred and some believe Memoria could be [[Primordial Magical Energy]].

Even though [[Talia the Weaver]] is usually someone who is more interconnected with the existence of Memoria, but also some other [[Demi-Gods]] alongside unknown [[Cult]] like organization that may exist all around the multiverse such as the mostly unknown and mysterious [[Enigmatic Group]], or [[Memory Burner]] which are a contrasts from the [[Memory Keeper]] who are also a group of unknown people who exist and believe to be related to the Memoria situation, but there are barely any prove or known fact about them.

#### Memoria Manipulation :
Although [[Memoria]] can be manipulated by other types of magic, as it is fundamentally a creation of humanoid psyche and cognition, those who can shift and weave Memoria are known as [[Memoria Wielder]]. These individuals manipulate Memoria for their own purposes.

Some are even capable of enhancing existing Memoria with other forms of power, such as [[Arcane Magical Energy]], [[Divine Magical Energy]], and even the more unusual and widely considered dangerous combination with [[Imaginatio]], a power based on imagination.

#### Memoria Creation :
Memoria are created by the form of heavy amount [[Emotional Weight]], and the [[Will of Ambition]] from many groups of induvial within an area, usually these heavy amount could only occurred during heavy amount of tragedy, as was recorded of the [[Shattered Knights]] founding themself many problem from Memoria as they live during the [[The Great Vallarian War]] which cause many amount of emotional distress which is why the [[Wayfarer]] of [[Talia the Weaver]] wishes to stop any mass incident or brutality to occur as if that would to happen, the danger of [[Memoria Bubble]] or a [[Memoria Wielder]] within the multiverse would be too dangerous. 

The tragedy that is needed to create Memoria also believe to effect the nature of the Memoria itself as some people within [[Intelligentsia Guild]] have created a theory about the nature of the Memoria behaviors towards the environment around them, but no one could prove them currently as there are only few recorded events of Memoria, and most of the Memoria that are created are usually from great tragedy which cause a high amount of [[Emotional Weight]] towards people. But even so the [[The Recollection Surveyor]] who is a an off shot from the [[Intelligentsia Guild]] by some people who wishes to use the power of Memoria to accomplishes the [[Mission of the Intelligentsia Guild]].
#### Different Type of Memoria :
Memoria has a two different Memoria that can be created from [[Emotional Weight]], and [[Will of Ambition]]. Which [[Memory Keeper]] simply called [[Positive Memoria]] where is from positive memories, and [[Negative Memoria]] where is from negative memories, and evil thoughts.

#### Meme Creature
[[Meme]] creature or [[Memetic creature]] sometime called are creatures that are created from Memoria, they themself are fully form out of Memoria. Although even so the [[Meme]] does not follow the same rule of many other [[Aberration]]  or [[Construct (Type)]] creature which usually does not have emotions or thinking capability other from rare incident, but the [[Meme]] creature that are seen are creatures who seem to able to think and sometime have emotions, but it does not mean every [[Meme]] creature can do it as these only apply to [[Natural Memetic Entity]].

Which are different from non-natural Memetic Entity which are creature which was created by [[Memoria Wielder]] which usually shape [[Memoria Bubble]] which is around them or sometime within them to form a [[Meme]] creature which for easier control are design to not able to think, even so these creatures are very powerful, as according to some records during them being an existence creature base upon power of recollection of past memories and emotions can transform into a creature that their Memoria are from which usually mean they became powerful being such as [[Fiends]] or even [[Humanoid]] of the past.
