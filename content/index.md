Welcome to the **Tales of Dasaron Wiki**, a collection of knowledge and lore for your own tales and adventures. 
Please remember to follow the [[Code of Conduct]] while exploring or contributing.

This is a homebrew world created by **[[Candle Note]]**, centered around the **[[Plane of Terra]]**.  
The world mainly revolves around three major continents:- 🌿 **[[Continent of Vallarian|Valla]]**, 🌊 **[[Titanfall Isles]]**, and 🌑 **[[The Lost Isles]]**

Here you will find guidance through the many paths that exist within **Tales of Dasaron**:
- 🧬 **[[Races & Species]]** — The many beings you may encounter or play as. With each having their own different [[Cultures]]
- 🏛️ **[[Gods & Goddess|Pantheons In Tales of Dasaron]]** — Gods and higher beings who shape the world
    - Including the [[Demi-Gods]] who move the “board” throughout the [[Timeline]]
- 📚Many [[Factions]] exist in Tales of Dasaron, some are of course [[Criminal Organization]], while some are [[Mysterious Organization]] that roam around the [[Multiverse]]
- 📝 **[[How to Write Your Backstory in Tales of Dasaron]]** — Learn how to create a character your [[Dungeon Master]] can easily weave into the story, and please check out the homebrew system that we use in [[Homebrew Rule|Homebrew Rules in Tales of Dasaron]]
